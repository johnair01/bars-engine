## The Three Dimensions of Human Experience

AQAL gives us three axes:

| Axis | What it distinguishes |
|------|----------------------|
| **Inner / Outer** | psychic vs social (what's mine vs what's ours) |
| **One / Many** | individual vs collective (solo vs group) |
| **Here / There** | immersion vs aspiration (player vs character) |

The third dimension is the one bars-engine has been working toward without naming it directly.

**Here** = the immersive world. You are inside it. It has you. You are not performing — you are *in it*.

**There** = the world you're striving toward. The not-yet. The character you're becoming.

**The Great There There** = where Here-UL and There-UL collapse into presence. Where player and character are the same thing. Where every compass points the same direction: toward what you can't name until you've gone through. The great mystery.

## The Eight Gates: Depth, Not Ladder

The gates are **levels of awakening**. Not a ladder — a going *down*. Into the body. Into what's already there before you named it.

Each gate is gated by a **threshold guardian** — a big mind voice that learned something and now holds the cost of what that opening means.

| Gate | Name | What the Guardian Asks |
|------|------|----------------------|
| 1 | **FEELING** | Can you feel what you actually feel? |
| 2 | **BELONGING** | Can you own your own belonging? |
| 3 | **LINES** | Can you draw the line and hold it? |
| 4 | **FIRE** | Can you use your anger without being used by it? |
| 5 | **GRIEF** | Can you lose what you came to keep? |
| 6 | **JOY** | Can you be excited without fearing what follows? |
| 7 | **SEEING** | Can you be seen without performing? |
| 8 | **SILENCE** | Can you stop choosing and just be? |

Gates are sphincters — body habits that close without your permission. The guardian behind each one is what learned what that opening costs. The work is not passing the gate. The work is meeting the guardian and answering what they ask.

---

## The Eight Spokes: Breath, Not Levels

The spokes are **types**, not depths. Equal. Cyclic. The 8 spokes of AQAL — each a line of development, each available at every gate level.

- Spokes breathe. Gates go down.
- Spokes are what circulate when the gates are open.
- A person can develop in one spoke while remaining closed in a gate.

## The Spatial Map: Village, Farm, Forest

```
VILLAGE → FARM → FOREST → FARM → VILLAGE
```

### The Village
- **Location in ontology:** Here-UR + There-UR (outer world, collective action)
- **Moves:** Clean Up + Show Up (outer work, execution)
- **What happens:** Allyship. Where people need you. Where change is demanded but the changer is unprepared.
- **The failure:** Trying to change the village without going through the forest.
- **The trap:** Staying in the village as a performing hero — always here, never going there.

### The Forest
- **Location in ontology:** Here-UL + There-UL (inner world, great there there)
- **Moves:** Wake Up + Grow Up (inner work, developmental)
- **What happens:** You find out where you are before you return to the village. Not the destination. Not a permanent camp. A necessary passage.
- **The failure:** Going to the forest and coming back better at being in the forest (anti-hero).
- **The trap:** Staying in the forest as an escape from the village.

*Tagline: "The village needs you. The forest prepared you. Going back is the work."*

### The Farm
- **Location in ontology:** The threshold between Here and There, between outer and inner
- **What it holds:** Liminal space. Where you land before entering the forest, and where you land after coming back. Where threshold guardians actually live — not inside the forest, but at the threshold between village and forest.
- **What it holds:** What guards the gate. Where you practice being with what you'd rather bypass. Where the big mind voice asks if you mean it — before you're deep enough to perform your way through.

## Cultivation as the Unnamed Framework

Bars-engine already has cultivation logic — it just hasn't used the word:

- **Qi Condensation** ≈ FEELING — sensing the charge before you name it
- **Foundation Establishment** ≈ BELONGING — building the base that holds everything
- **Core Formation** ≈ LINES — the boundary that defines what you will and won't do
- **Nascent Soul** ≈ FIRE — anger as the first fully-owned energy
- **Immortal Ascension** ≈ GRIEF — the death before the rebirth
- **Post-Ascension** ≈ JOY — joy as a cultivated state, not a reaction
- **Tribulation** ≈ SEEING — being seen by something larger than yourself
- **Return to the Mortal** ≈ SILENCE — going back without having to perform

The five nations map to cultivation paths:
- **Argyra (Metal/Fear)** — precision, form, the path of clarity through confrontation
- **Virelune (Wood/Joy)** — growth, expansion, the path of becoming through vitality
- **Pyrakanth (Fire/Excitement)** — transformation, the path through burning and tending
- **Meridia** — ?
- **Lamenth** — ?

Each nation is a different way of relating to the same eight gates. The spatial ecology ("Fear seed needs proximity to Joy to evolve") is already cultivation logic — different elements that can only transform in relationship to each other.
