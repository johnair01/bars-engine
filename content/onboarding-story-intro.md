# Onboarding Story Intro

Canonical story-world intro copy for chained initiation. Leads with Conclave/heist. Tone: comedic heist (Ocean's 11) + Hitchhiker's Guide wit. Source: [story_context.md](../docs/handbook/world/story_context.md), [lore-index.md](./lore-index.md).

## Principle

Show through story, not mechanics. Don't explain nations → tell nation stories that players want to inhabit. Players enter the world before they choose.

---

## Intro Beats (for packet compiler)

### Beat 1 — Orientation
The Conclave has convened. Five nations. A heist at the Robot Oscars. Giant constructs powered by emotional energy. You're walking in mid-formation — and your participation matters.

### Beat 2 — Rising Engagement
Each nation channels a different emotional current: Argyra's clarity, Pyrakanth's fire, Virelune's hope, Meridia's calm, Lamenth's flow. The crew is assembling. The heist needs every kind of pilot.

### Beat 3 — Tension
The stakes are real. A corrupt force threatens construct technology. The Oscars bring the elite out of hiding — and something valuable is there for the taking. The gap between where we are and where we're going? That's the heist.

### Beat 4 — Integration
You're not filling out a form. You're joining the crew. Choose your nation, your approach, your role. The threshold is near.
