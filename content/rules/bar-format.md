# BAR Format: Brevity + Quadrant

When you publish a public BAR, format matters. Here's how to shape your signal so it lands.

## Quadrant (Human Language)

Tag your BAR by what it's about:

| Quadrant | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **About me** | Personal insight, feeling, intention |
| **About something happening** | Observable event, situation, pattern |
| **About us** | Relational, cultural, collective |
| **About the system** | Structural, institutional, how things work |

Quadrant helps others find and play your BAR. No AQAL jargon—just plain language.

## Brevity

Public BARs should be **short**. Compress. One idea, one seed. The spellbook rewards clarity over length.

## Optional: Tags, Yes/And

- **Tags** — Add keywords for discovery
- **Yes/And** — Build on others' BARs; add rather than replace

Avoid forcing "I feel" syntax. The format is flexible; the constraint is brevity and quadrant.
