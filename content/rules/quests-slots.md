# Quests + BAR Slots + Minting

Quests are actionable commitments. BARs play into them. Completion mints vibeulons.

## Fixed Slots

Each quest has a **fixed slot count**. Slots are claimed first-come, first-served (FCFS). When a slot is filled, the BAR is "in play" for that quest.

## Minting on Completion

When you **complete a quest**, each BAR played on it mints **1 vibeulon**. Provenance links the vibeulon to the BAR and the quest. Allocation goes to the BAR's steward.

## The Loop

1. Equip BARs
2. Play BARs onto quest slots (FCFS)
3. Complete the quest
4. Each played BAR → 1 vibeulon (to steward)
5. Provenance: BAR + quest recorded
