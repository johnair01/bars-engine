# Anonymity + Stewardship Adoption

You can post BARs anonymously, and anyone can adopt stewardship of a public BAR. Stewardship is practice—carrying, refining, upgrading, converting to quest, composting.

## Anonymous BARs

Toggle **anonymous** per BAR when creating or editing. Anonymous BARs don't show your identity. The signal stands on its own.

## Stewardship Adoption

- **Anyone can adopt** — Stewardship does not claim authorship; it claims responsibility for carrying the BAR
- **Persistence** — Stewardship lasts until you release it or the BAR is composted
- **Practice** — Stewarding means: carrying, refining, upgrading, converting to quest, composting with intention

Stewardship is not ownership. It's the practice of tending signal—yours or someone else's—until it transforms or returns to the fire.
