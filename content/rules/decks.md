# Decks: Library / Equipped / In Play

BARs move through zones. Understanding where they live helps you manage your deck.

## Zones

| Zone | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Library (spellbook)** | All public BARs you steward |
| **Equipped (hand)** | Curated subset for current play; capacity limits apply |
| **In Play** | Attached to a quest slot |
| **Compost** | Removed from circulation; temporary; can be transformed or destroyed |
| **Destroyed** | Gone permanently |

## Key Rule: Equipped Does Nothing Passively

Equipped BARs sit in your hand. They **do nothing** until you actively play them onto a quest. Scarcity lives in equipped capacity—you can't hold everything. Choose what to carry.

## Flow

Library → Equip (curate) → Play (attach to quest) → Complete quest (mint) or Compost (remove) → Transform or Destroy
