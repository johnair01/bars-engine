# Rules Glossary

Quick definitions for the BAR/Quest/Vibeulon loop.

- **BAR** — Basic Artifact Resource. A compact, shareable unit of signal—a "seed packet" for eventual action. Can be private (notebook) or public (spellbook).

- **Vibeulon** — A unit of crystallized value minted from completing quests. Can attach to BARs (locking them). Destroyed if left attached when compost expires.

- **Quest** — An actionable commitment in the field. Has limited BAR slots; BARs played onto quests mint vibeulons on completion.

- **Stewardship** — Responsibility for carrying a BAR. Anyone can adopt; does not claim authorship. Persists until released or composted.

- **Compost** — A zone for BARs removed from active play. Temporary; untransformed composted BARs are destroyed after a time window.

- **Equipped** — BARs in your hand—curated for current play. Capacity limits apply. Equipped BARs do nothing passively.

- **In Play** — BAR attached to a quest slot.

- **Quadrant** — Tag for BAR content: About me, About something happening, About us, About the system.
