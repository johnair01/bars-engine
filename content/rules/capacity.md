# Capacity + Refinement Progression

Your **equipped** limit (hand size) starts small. It expands as you refine.

## Capacity = Equipped Limit

Capacity is how many BARs you can hold in your hand (equipped) at once. Scarcity lives here. You can't carry everything.

## Refinement Progression

Capacity **expands** via refinement actions:

- Merging BARs
- Forking BARs
- Upgrading BARs
- Converting BARs to quests
- Composting with lesson

Repeated refinement grows your capacity. Deck management is a core skill.

## The Progression

Start small. Refine. Your hand expands. The more you tend the garden—merge, fork, upgrade, convert, compost—the more you can hold.
