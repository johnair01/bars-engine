# Compost Heap: Clean Up + Destruction

Compost is where BARs go when they leave active play. It's temporary. Untransformed compost expires—and then destruction happens.

## Composting

- **Removes BAR from deck** — No longer equipped or playable
- **Cannot be equipped or played** — Until transformed
- **Can be adopted/transformed** — Stewardship and transformation still apply

## Transformation Required to Re-Enter

To get a composted BAR back into play, you must **transform** it: merge, fork, or rewrite. Raw compost doesn't return. Transformation is the gate.

## Expiration + Destruction

Composted BARs that are **not transformed** before expiry are **destroyed**. The BAR and any attached vibeulons are gone. Destruction is logged publicly.

## Tone

Ecological, not moralistic. "Returned to the fire" language is allowed. Neglected charge burns—that's the design. The system metabolizes stagnation.
