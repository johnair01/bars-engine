# Slot Offers: Merge / Buyout + Public Override

When a quest slot is filled, you can propose displacement—if you have a better-suited BAR. Slot offers are voluntary, public, and time-bound.

## Voluntary Withdrawal

The incumbent can **withdraw** voluntarily. Cost: **1 vibeulon** (burned). The slot opens for others.

## Displacement Options

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Merge Offer** | Merge incumbent + proposed BAR into a transformed BAR |
| **Buyout / Seat Transfer** | Offer vibeulons to incumbent to vacate the slot |

## Public + Transparent

- Offers are **public** — Attunement note, countdown visible
- **One active offer per slot** — No stacking
- **Escrow** — Vibeulons held until resolution
- **Response window** — Default ~12 hours; no response → time-based override (auto-execute)

## Consent + Flow

Tone: consent and flow, not forced seizure. The system favors voluntary movement. Time override exists so slots don't stall forever.
