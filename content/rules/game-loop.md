# The Game Loop

The core loop is simple: **Capture BARs → Extend to Quests → Play → Complete → Mint Vibeulons.**

## Four Steps

1. **Capture** — When something feels charged, capture it. A few words is enough. Go to [Capture](/capture) or create a BAR from the dashboard.
2. **Extend** — Turn your charge into a quest. Use "Explore" on a captured charge to see suggested quests, then create one. BARs become quests; quests become action.
3. **Play** — Equip BARs, play them onto quest slots. Campaign quests live on the [Gameboard](/campaign/board?ref=bruised-banana)—that's where you complete them.
4. **Complete** — Finish the quest. Each BAR played on it mints **1 vibeulon**. Provenance links the vibeulon to the BAR and the quest.

## The Four Moves

How you get things done depends on which move you're in:

| Move | Meaning | Where |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Wake Up** | See more of what's available (who, what, where, how) | [Library](/library) |
| **Clean Up** | Get more emotional energy; unblock vibeulon-generating actions | [Emotional First Aid](/emotional-first-aid) |
| **Grow Up** | Increase skill capacity through developmental lines | [Schools / Dojos](/dojos) |
| **Show Up** | Do the work of completing quests | [Gameboard](/campaign/board?ref=bruised-banana) |

The [Game Map](/game-map) shows all four lobbies. Each lobby maps to one move.

## Campaign Quests

Campaign quests (from the Bruised Banana residency campaign) **complete on the Gameboard**. When you pick up a campaign quest, go to the Gameboard to finish it. Personal quests and BAR-derived quests can be completed from your dashboard or wallet.
