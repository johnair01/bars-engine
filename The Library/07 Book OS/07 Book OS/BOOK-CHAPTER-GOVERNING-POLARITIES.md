### Ch3 — Force ↔ Restraint

**Review spec:** `../../manuscripts/sources/integral-design/chapter-polarities/ch3-challenger/CH3_POLARITY_REVIEW_SPEC_2026-05-28.md`

| | **Force** | **Restraint** |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| **Healthy** | Charge, line, interrupt, refusal, decisive protection | Aim, containment, cost awareness, clean exit, no unnecessary damage |
| **Shadow** | Domination, venting, punishment, confrontation as identity | Appeasement, apology-loop, rehearsed scripts, waiting until the line dissolves |

**Tension:** A clean no needs enough force to be real and enough restraint to remain clean.

**Status note:** Current provisional naming is under review because it may not be neutral enough for the ideal reader; do not treat this pair as settled.

### Ch4 — Honor ↔ Reform

| | **Honor** | **Reform** |
|---|-----------|------------|
| **Healthy** | Loyalty, continuity, memory, vows, protecting what still works | Renewal, adaptation, repair, changing the form to preserve the essence |
| **Shadow** | Compliance, rigidity, role captivity, protecting harm because it is old | Replacement reflex, contempt for lineage, reform as self-exemption |

**Tension:** Traditions worth protecting and traditions worth breaking are often the same tradition at different layers.

### Ch5 — Design ↔ Handoff

| | **Design** | **Handoff** |
|---|------------|-------------|
| **Healthy** | Structure, leverage, clarity, making the right thing easy | Transfer, accessibility, successor-readiness, structural generosity |
| **Shadow** | Control, elegance as monument, indispensability, over-engineering | Abandonment, vague delegation, underbuilt systems, responsibility dump |

**Tension:** A good structure has to work, and it has to leave the next person more capable rather than more dependent.

### Ch6 — Field ↔ Terms

| | **Field** | **Terms** |
|---|-----------|-----------|
| **Healthy** | Attunement, hearing, bridge, repair, enough safety for truth | Stakes, closure, agreement, what staying requires, movement that holds |
| **Shadow** | Field-management, over-accommodation, everyone-heard-nothing-moves | Ultimatum, leverage-as-care, legalism, closure before the truth is held |

**Tension:** Everyone can be heard and something can still move; if either side disappears, the Diplomat collapses into harmony theater or coercion.

**Relation to GP-A1:** Care ↔ Impact remains the Ch6 practice dialect. Field ↔ Terms is the chapter-level governing pair.

### Ch7 — Altitude ↔ Ground

| | **Altitude** | **Ground** |
|---|--------------|------------|
| **Healthy** | Pattern recognition, panoramic view, altitude diagnosis, naming the game | Service, embodiment, specific move, dignity for the people still inside the game |
| **Shadow** | Detachment, superiority, meta-view as status, narrating instead of serving | Engulfment, localism, altitude allergy, action without pattern |

**Tension:** Seeing the whole matters only if it helps you return more precisely to the part that needs you.
