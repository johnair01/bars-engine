# Comedic + Source Integration Matrix

## Decisions Locked 2026-06-02

- **Direct quote density:** for academic integrity, target **2-3 direct quotes per chapter**. The rest of the source work should be inline attribution, paraphrase, and back-matter mapping.
- **I Ching acknowledgment:** belongs in **Ch0** when the oracle/card mechanic is introduced. It may also appear in deck front matter and back matter, but Ch0 is the required manuscript location.
- **Polarity lineage:** attribute polarity thinking/mapping to **Barry Johnson** and **Beena Sharma**, especially Sharma's Integral Life course *Integrating Polarities* and her work with Johnson / Polarity Wisdom.
- **Council of Joanne's:** author-side antagonist system only. Do not name the Council directly in manuscript prose unless Wendell explicitly changes this. Use it as a diagnostic and punch-up engine.
- **Jerk register:** may be **very sharp when earned**. Wendell will flag excess. The pass should not pre-soften the Jerk voice out of fear.

## Purpose

This matrix turns two open editorial needs into one pass:

1. **Comedic calibration:** use humor to metabolize shame, not decorate prose.
2. **Source integration:** name the book's intellectual lineage without making the chapters feel academic.

The operating principle: **sources provide ground; comedy provides relief; Wendell voice metabolizes both.**

This is not a punch-up pass and not a citation pass. It is a lineage-and-relief pass.

---

## Pass Rules

- Do not add jokes to teaching-mode exposition.
- Do not add citations as credentials.
- Do not quote a source where Wendell's embodied voice needs to lead.
- Put sources near conceptual hinges, not inside the hottest wound unless the source creates relief.
- Use direct quotes deliberately: **2-3 per chapter** as the target integrity layer, with the rest paraphrased or acknowledged in Wendell's voice.
- Keep the reader's body in the room. If a quote moves the prose into seminar mode, cut it or move it to back matter.
- Comedy should reveal the shame parasite and leave the raw signal intact.
- Treat the Council of Joanne's as an author-side antagonist diagnostic. The reader should feel the force; the manuscript does not need to name the system.
- Let the Jerk be sharp when the passage has earned it. The safety mechanism is placement and Wendell review, not pre-emptive softening.

---

## Integration Modes

| Mode | Use When | Shape |
|---|---|---|
| Inline attribution | A framework first appears or a source debt needs to be named | "Elliott gave me the cleanest version of this..." |
| Quote hinge | A source sentence says the thing better and shorter than paraphrase | short quote -> Wendell translates -> practice continues |
| Back-matter citation | The source is real but would slow the chapter | mention in "Maps I Used" / Sources |
| Comic bridge | The source is serious and the reader needs relief after it | source claim -> absurd consequence -> grounded practice |
| Confessional metabolizer | The reader may feel accused | Wendell takes the hit first, then source/frame lands |

## Output Format for the Actual Pass

For each proposed insertion or revision, produce:

```markdown
## ChN -- <section / anchor>

**Purpose:** What charge this metabolizes.
**Comic archetype:** Clown / Cult Leader / Jerk / self-immolation.
**Source lineage:** Source and why it belongs.
**Risk:** Low / Medium / High / Very high.
**Current passage:** short anchor only.
**Proposed edit:** draft prose.
**Why this works:** one paragraph.
**Approval:** pending / approved / rejected / revise.
```

No canonical chapter edits until Wendell approves the proposed edit.
