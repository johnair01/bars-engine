# Ch6 Prose Rhythm Pass

## Purpose

Resolve the repeated template sentence **This is a costly move** after the Ch5-Ch8 energy-language sweep.

The concept was correct, but the repetition made the Diplomat's capacity language sound mechanical. Ch6 needs to preserve cost, terms, and depletion without sounding like a renamed point system.

## Changes Made

Revised the repeated cost sentences so each one names the specific kind of cost native to the channel:

- Bridge-Builder: bridging has a real cost; name it before warmth turns into depletion.
- Translator: the price is precision.
- Field-Holder: staying spends real capacity; unnamed depletion becomes disappearance.
- Repairer: the cost is time; slower repair is what makes repair trustworthy.
- Integrative Negotiator: the hard part is the close.
- False Closure: discernment asks more than comfortable vagueness.

Also synced the stronger embodied line:

> Resentful peace sits as heaviness; honest terms feel like heat with ground.
