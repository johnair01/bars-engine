## The Adaptation

The 6 unpacking questions are a personality-part coaching protocol. This adapts them for **book editing as an EA intake tool** — the creation being developed is the reader's transformation, not a separate product.

**Key shift:** The 6 questions answer from the author's perspective about the reader, not from a personality part's perspective about itself. The questions become the author's editorial intake: *what EA state am I moving this reader through, and what has to be true for that to land?*

---

## The 6 Editorial Intake Questions

### Q1 — What do you want to create for the reader?
**Q1 reframe:** What reader experience do you want this section to produce?

The author's answer names the target experience — what the reader should feel, know, or be able to do after the section. This is the destination.

### Q2 — What will that get them?
**Q2 reframe:** What EA satisfaction state does the reader arrive at?

The EA outcome. The state they're in after the transformation lands. Satisfaction, peace, triumph, grounding, purpose, connection — the specific EA channel being activated by this section's work.

### Q3 — What's life like right now for the reader?
**Q3 reframe:** What is the reader's current reality before this section?

Describe the reader's actual situation — the context they're living in before the section changes anything. This is neutral, observational, specific. Not what you wish they felt. What they actually feel, doing the actual work.

### Q4 — How does it feel to live here?
**Q4 reframe:** What is the reader's current dissatisfaction state?

The gap between where the reader is (Q3) and where they want to be (Q2). The specific dissatisfaction the EA move in this section will address. Named precisely — which EA channel, which emotion.

### Q5 — What would have to be true for someone to feel this way?
**Q5 reframe:** What wisdom does this section deliver? What has to be true for the reader to arrive at the target state?

The content. The specific truth, move, or reframe the section must deliver for the EA transformation to land. This is what the section actually says. The wisdom that closes the gap from Q3 to Q2.

### Q6 — What reservations do you have about your creation?
**Q6 reframe:** Which of the 6 self-sabotaging beliefs blocks this reader from receiving this section?

The author's reservation about writing the section — but also the reader's likely reservation about receiving it. These map to the 6 beliefs:
1. I'm not enough
2. I'm too much
3. I don't fit
4. I don't belong
5. I'm not ready
6. I don't know how

## How It Interfaces with EA

```
Q1 (creation intention)      → names the target EA state
Q2 (what it gets them)       → the satisfaction move (outcome)
Q3 (current reality)         → the neutral starting point
Q4 (how it feels to live there) → the dissatisfaction (Dissatisfaction)
Q5 (what has to be true)     → the Transcend move (the alchemical content)
Q6 (reservations)            → the specific belief the section must address
```

**The EA transformation flow through Q1-6:**
- Q1 → target state
- Q4 → starting dissatisfaction
- Q5 → the Transcend move that converts Q4→Q2
- Q6 → the belief that could block the move from landing

## Canonical EA Emotional Channels (Source: Ch5 Architect draft, line 120

| EA Channel | What the emotion signal is detecting | Transcend outcome |
|-----------|-------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Metal/Fear** | Threat in awareness — risk, something that could break, boundary of the known | → Excitement (risk known and mapped) |
| **Water/Sadness** | Loss — something cared about that is far away or gone; what worked once and doesn't anymore | → Poignancy (honoring what was while releasing it) |
| **Wood/Joy** | Vitality — what wants to exist but doesn't yet; delight in something present | → Bliss (the thing exists, aliveness confirmed) |

| **Earth/Neutrality** | Detachment — observing from a distance, systems analysis, crossroads | → Peace (clarity without charge) |

## What Each EA Emotion Means (Precise Definitions)

**Fear (Metal):** The nervous system has detected a boundary — a threat at the edge of the known. Something that could break. The signal says: *be careful here, there is risk*.

**Sadness (Water):** Something is being grieved — something cared about that is far away, lost, or no longer present. The signal says: *I care about this and it is not here*.

**Joy (Wood):** Vitality detected — something is alive, growing, present, working. The signal says: *this is working, this is here, this is real*.

**Anger (Fire):** An obstacle is detected that needs to be moved or overcome. Something is in the way. The signal says: *this should not be here, remove it*.

**Neutrality (Earth):** The system is being observed from outside — analytical, detached, at a crossroads. The signal says: *I can see the whole structure from here*.

## Q5 in EA Terms — "What Would Have to Be True?"

Q5 asks for the specific truth that must land in the reader for the EA transformation to occur.

**When the dissatisfaction is Fear (Metal):** Q5 asks what truth must the reader learn about the threat/risk. What must be mapped? What must they become aware of?

**When the dissatisfaction is Anger (Fire):** Q5 asks what obstacle must be named. What must be moved. The truth of *what exactly is in the way*.

**When the dissatisfaction is Sadness (Water):** Q5 asks what loss must be honored. What used to work. The truth of *what is being grieved*.

**When the dissatisfaction is Joy (Wood):** Q5 asks what vitality must be confirmed. What is working. The truth of *what is real and present*.

**When the dissatisfaction is Neutrality (Earth):** Q5 asks what structure must be seen. The whole system from outside. The truth of *what the pattern is*.

**Q5 is the Transcend move content — the specific wisdom that converts the reader's Q4 dissatisfaction into Q2 satisfaction.**

## Usage in the WAVE Editing Spiral

**Wake Up phase:** Run Q1-6 per section before writing. The answers produce:
- The section's editorial intention (Q1)
- The EA move being deployed (Q4→Q5→Q2)
- The specific reservation to watch for in the reader (Q6)

**Clean Up phase:** Does the written section actually deliver Q5? Does Q6 show up in any hedging, avoiding, or over-promising?

**Grow Up phase:** Does the section operate at a higher level? (voice, precision, structural integrity)

**Show Up phase:** Write from the Q1-6 answers. Edit against them.
