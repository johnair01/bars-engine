# School Rank Matrix - Altitudes Within Each School

Status: v0.4 design draft

Purpose:

Each School is horizontal, but its ranks express the six Faces as altitude interpretations inside that School.

## Universal Rank Ladder

| Rank | Altitude / Face | What It Means Inside Any School |
|---|---|---|
| Initiate | Shaman | Feel the School's signal before explaining it. |
| Practitioner | Challenger | Act through the School under pressure. |
| Adept | Regent | Preserve and repeat the School's practice responsibly. |
| Steward | Architect | Design systems that make the School's practice scalable. |
| Integrator | Diplomat | Use the School's practice across relationship and difference. |
| Keeper | Sage | Hold the School's whole game and teach it in service. |

## Design Consequence

This matrix lets campaign packets feature both:

- a School focus
- an altitude focus

Example:

A campaign may focus on the School of the Bridge at the Practitioner / Challenger rank. That means the story is not only "diplomacy." It is specifically about the courage to say the hard true thing while staying in relationship.

Another campaign may focus on the School of the Body at the Steward / Architect rank. That means the story is about designing reliable containers for embodied wisdom, not just feeling things.

This creates many campaign products without needing new core mechanics each time.
