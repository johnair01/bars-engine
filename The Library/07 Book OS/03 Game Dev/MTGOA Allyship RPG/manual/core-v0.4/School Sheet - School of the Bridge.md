# School Sheet - School of the Bridge

Face: **Diplomat**

Owned Stat: **Tend**

Milestone question:

**Can you hold relationship without vanishing?**

Core lesson:

Real bridges stand on both sides.

## Vibe

The School of the Bridge occupies tea rooms, negotiation decks, translation chambers, conflict circles, guest quarters, and liminal hallways where enemies can become complicated.

It feels like water tribe diplomacy, X-Men moral disagreement, coalition organizing, peace talks, and learning the terms under which you will walk away.

## Head of School

Name: **Head Elian Cross**

Title: Keeper of the Walk-Away Terms

What they embody:

Connection without self-abandonment.

Teaching style:

Elian makes students state what they believe, what the other side needs, and what would make staying dishonest.

Blind spot:

They can stay too long in rooms that have already chosen harm.

Signature line:

"If you vanish to keep the bridge, there is no bridge."

## Lieutenants

### Nima Vale

Title: Translator of Heat

Subskill: Emotional undercurrent detection

What they teach:

Students learn to hear what a room is emotionally negotiating beneath the stated agenda.

How they test students:

Nima runs conversations where the literal words are polite and the emotional truth is not.

Shadow version:

Mind-reading instead of checking.

Move or training prompt:

**Name the Undertow:** When you name the emotional current in a room as a question, not a verdict, take +1 forward to Wake Up.

### Aric Fen

Title: Coalition Tutor

Subskill: Alignment across difference

What they teach:

Students learn to build limited agreements between people who do not share the same worldview.

How they test students:

Aric gives students three factions who agree on the problem and disagree on the reason.

Shadow version:

False unity.

Move or training prompt:

**Find the Shared Yes:** When you identify a specific action two opposed parties can both support for different reasons, gain 1 Leverage.

### Sola Min

Title: Boundary Mediator

Subskill: Staying without self-erasure

What they teach:

Students learn to remain in relationship while naming their own position.

How they test students:

Sola pairs students with someone they want to appease and asks them to disagree plainly.

Shadow version:

Fawning disguised as compassion.

Move or training prompt:

**Disagree and Stay:** When you name disagreement without attacking or disappearing, roll + Grow Up. On a hit, Trust can survive the disagreement.

## Trial

The student must hold a bridge between two valid perspectives without splitting the difference or abandoning their own truth.

They pass if a third possibility appears.

## Shadow Exam

The student is asked to preserve peace by swallowing the truth.

They pass by naming the truth and the relationship they still want to protect.

## Mark / Badge

The **Crossroad Pin**, worn at the collar or heart.

## Move Unlock: Hold the Bridge

When you keep contact across real difference, roll + Clean Up or + Grow Up.

On a 10+, choose 2.

On a 7-9, choose 1.

On a 6-, the bridge holds only if you abandon something important. Say what you refuse to abandon.

Options:

- A relationship survives a hard truth.
- A false agreement falls away.
- A third possibility appears.
- Someone names what they need to continue.

## Downtime Training

During downtime, train with the School of the Bridge to:

- repair a strained Bond
- clarify walk-away terms
- rehearse a hard conversation
- reduce Faction Heat by creating a limited agreement
- ask what you are smoothing over

## Campaign Use

Use this School when:

- a mission involves coalition tension
- the table is confusing peace with alignment
- characters need to disagree without rupture
- a faction relationship is more important than a single win
