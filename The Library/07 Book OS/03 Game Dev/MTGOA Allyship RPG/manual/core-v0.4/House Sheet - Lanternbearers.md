# House Sheet - Lanternbearers

Domain: **Raise Awareness**

Core question:

**What truth must become visible, and with whose consent?**

## Vibe

Witnesses, storytellers, signal keepers, archivists, journalists, mirror-workers, people who know a hidden pattern can run a whole society until someone makes it legible.

Lanternbearers are the House of careful visibility.

They reveal what has been trained into silence.

## Motto

**Make it visible. Do not make them a symbol.**

## Symbol

A lantern with an eye-shaped flame.

## Gift

Lanternbearers can name what everyone feels but no one has language for.

They understand the difference between witness and exposure.

## Shadow

Lanternbearers can turn truth into spectacle.

They may chase the dramatic reveal, overexpose people, confuse discourse with change, or use visibility as a substitute for support.

Shadow tells:

- "People need to know."
- "If we name it, we fixed it."
- "This story will move people."
- "Awareness is the work."

## Known For

- pattern naming
- public witness
- story stewardship
- consent checks
- archives
- symbolic action
- making the invisible legible

## What They Get Wrong

- exposing people without consent
- turning pain into proof
- valuing story over material change
- mistaking virality for accountability
- narrating other people's lives too quickly

## Starting Move: Light the Pattern

When you make a hidden pattern visible, roll + Wake Up or + Show Up.

On a 10+, choose 2.

On a 7-9, choose 1 and ask who becomes more exposed.

On a 6-, the truth becomes visible, but the wrong person pays first.

Options:

- The pattern becomes undeniable.
- You protect the dignity of the affected.
- You identify who must consent before the story spreads.
- A false public story loses power.
- Gain 1 Clarity.

## House Feature: Consent Before Signal

Once per episode, before information becomes public, you may pause the table and ask:

**Who becomes more vulnerable if this becomes known?**

If the table changes course because of the answer, restore 1 Trust with an affected person or faction.

## House Clock

**Spectacle Spiral**

1. A hidden truth is sensed.
2. The most dramatic version spreads first.
3. Affected people lose control of the story.
4. Helpers gather around the visibility.
5. Material support lags behind attention.
6. The story becomes famous and the people become exhausted.

## Relationship to Other Houses

Provisioners:

Lanternbearers need Provisioners so awareness leads to support. They fear quiet care can leave patterns unnamed.

Weavers:

Lanternbearers need Weavers to turn insight into structure. They resent process that buries truth.

Linekeepers:

Lanternbearers need Linekeepers when naming is not enough. They fear action without narrative consent.

## House Advancement Prompt

When you finish a mission, ask:

**Who became more visible, and did they choose it?**

If your answer protects consent in the next scene, mark Growth.
