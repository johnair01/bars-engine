# Manual Product Brief - v0.2

Product: **Mastering the Game of Allyship: The Living City Roleplaying Game**

Artifact type: digital tabletop RPG manual / zine / printable play kit.

## Strategic Role

The manual is the low-friction entry product in the MTGOA value stack.

Running a facilitated allyship campaign is high-value but expensive. A roleplaying manual is curiosity-priced. It lets nerds, facilitators, organizers, educators, and friend groups encounter the framework as a playable object before they buy deeper support.

## Core Buyer Promise

**Practice allyship before the moment costs someone real.**

The buyer gets a game they can read, run, hack, argue about, and bring to a table.

## Target Buyers

- tabletop RPG players curious about social practice games
- facilitators who want a safer training frame than roleplay exercises
- educators and organizers who want a game-based entry point
- coaches and community builders
- MTGOA readers who want a concrete practice artifact
- friend groups who like intense, meaningful play

## Product Shape

Minimum digital product:

- PDF manual
- character sheet
- map sheet
- Chronicle sheet
- move reference
- starter Episode deck
- starter Static deck
- one-shot module: The Missing Water

Expanded digital product:

- four-session campaign packet
- printable Role cards
- printable Motive cards
- printable Crisis Clock sheet
- facilitator guide
- solo reflection mode

Physical product:

- zine or perfect-bound manual
- card deck
- folded city map
- character pads
- campaign journal

## Value Stack Placement

1. Free / low-cost:
   - essay excerpts
   - one-page quickstart
   - sample Episode

2. Entry paid:
   - digital manual and print kit

3. Mid-tier:
   - physical book / zine
   - card deck
   - campaign expansion

4. Premium:
   - guided campaigns
   - facilitator training
   - organizational scenario design
   - custom allyship campaign for a group

## Why It Works

The manual makes MTGOA tangible.

It changes the offer from:

"Pay me to teach you allyship."

to:

"Here is a game artifact. Play it and discover what your allyship does under pressure."

That is more inviting, more shareable, and more culturally native to the nerd curiosity spend.
