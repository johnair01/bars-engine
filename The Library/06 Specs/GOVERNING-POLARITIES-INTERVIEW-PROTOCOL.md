# Governing Polarities Interview Protocol

**Purpose:** Extract **governing polarities** (living teaching tensions) from Wendell — separate from structural deck-grid polarity (Scene Atlas, Gate×Chapter).

**Not in scope:** Naming pair1/pair2 for engine. That is Phase B, after this protocol.

**Research context:** External polarity frameworks summarized in `POLARITY-THINKING-RESEARCH.md`. Wendell's FSR Polarity Rules (`../FSR/Polarity Rules.md`) are the **governing** audit bar — stricter than Johnson alone.

**If formal mapping rules remain unclear after research:** Use § Formal mapping addendum in `POLARITY-THINKING-RESEARCH.md` §6 during or after Phase B.

---

## Before session

- [ ] Facilitator read `POLARITY-TYPES-CANON.md`
- [ ] Wendell: optional 321 somatic practice ([book editing protocol](https://wendellbritt.zo.space/321))
- [ ] Pick **one product line** per session (do not mix allyship + friendcraft in one hour)
- [ ] Open `GOVERNING-POLARITIES-REGISTER.md` for logging

## Cast statement (read aloud)

> We're not asking what labels go on a grid. We're asking: **what tensions do you teach inside this craft** — tensions that live in you and in the people you're writing for — where leaning too hard on one pole breaks the practice?

---

## Core questions (all lines)

1. **What do people get wrong** when they think they're doing [allyship / friendship] well?
2. **What do they optimize for** that secretly undermines the thing?
3. **Name two poles** they swing between — not good/bad, but **both real, both insufficient alone**.
4. **Where do you sit** when you're at your best? When you're burned out?
5. **What would a card that only affirms one pole** feel like — and why would you reject it?
6. **Is there a third thing** people confuse with one of the poles? (e.g. performance care vs real care)

---

## Allyship supplement (after core)

- How does **care ↔ impact** show up in each Show Up domain?
- Relation to **debt vs gift** (Ch0 Infinite Arcade)?
- Relation to **courtroom vs practice** allyship?
- Does **care** ever masquerade as Clean Up (shadow integration without action)?

---

## Friendcraft supplement (after core)

- How does **safety ↔ growth** relate to **graduation** (game wins when practice ends)?
- Relation to **provenance vs stats**?
- Is **async safety** (text friendship) a shadow form of safety pole?
- What **locale** fits friendship journey — forest or somewhere else? (narrative only, not hexagram copy)
