# 321 Capture Worksheet

## Purpose

Turn the card encounter into a 3rd-person, 2nd-person, and 1st-person practice that deepens recognition without overcomplicating the deck.

## Worksheet Sections

### 3rd Person: Face It

What is this part, pattern, or voice trying to do from the outside?

### 2nd Person: Speak To It

Ask the voice what it protects, fears, wants, or refuses.

### 1st Person: Become It

Complete: "I keep you safe by..." and "What I need you to understand is..."

### Integration

What changes when this voice is treated as signal instead of proof against you?
