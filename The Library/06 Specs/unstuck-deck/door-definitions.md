## Door Working Definitions

### Shaman

The Shaman Door is for stuckness organized around felt sense, charge, shadow, and the signal before the story. The gift is attunement. The distortion is over-attunement: reading everything, carrying too much, waiting for perfect clarity, or mistrusting a real signal because it arrives through sensation before explanation.

Cards in this Door should help the player separate real signal from projection, panic, old pattern, and other people's material. The Shaman card job is not to make the player less sensitive. It is to make sensitivity usable.

**Write against:** signal, charge, felt sense, shadow, underworld, projection, timing, embodied knowing.

**Avoid:** treating sensitivity as pathology, making the player responsible for everyone else's state, vague mysticism without a practice move.

### Challenger

The Challenger Door is for stuckness organized around truth, friction, resistance, and the cost of confrontation. The gift is clean force. The distortion is edited truth: hedging, softening, sharpening into attack, or delaying confrontation because the truth might change the field.

Cards in this Door should help the player name the true thing with precision instead of politeness theater or reactivity. The Challenger card job is not to make the player more aggressive. It is to restore clean contact with what is real.

**Write against:** truth, edge, confrontation, precision, false consensus, resistance, courage, decisive action.

**Avoid:** contrarianism for its own sake, moral superiority, confusing aggression with clarity.

### Regent

The Regent Door is for stuckness organized around stewardship, duty, continuity, and over-responsibility. The gift is devotion to what matters. The distortion is heroic bracing: holding the center so completely that the system never learns to hold.

Cards in this Door should help the player distinguish earned responsibility from inherited burden, clarify roles and boundaries, and build structure that preserves trust without requiring constant personal sacrifice.

**Write against:** stewardship, governance, duty, continuity, trust, boundary, role, dignity, what must be preserved.

**Avoid:** glamorizing martyrdom, rigid control disguised as responsibility, collapsing care into obligation.

### Architect

The Architect Door is for stuckness organized around systems, mechanisms, plans, and design. The gift is structured creation. The distortion is protective over-design: building more safeguards, maps, flows, and frameworks to avoid the risk of letting the work touch reality.

Cards in this Door should help the player convert design into contact: a test, a sequence, a decision rule, a feedback loop, or a smaller structure that can actually meet the world.

**Write against:** systems, sequence, mechanism, design, execution, feedback, ambiguity, decision flow, reality test.

**Avoid:** tool chasing, abstraction as delay, building features before contact with the living problem.

### Diplomat

The Diplomat Door is for stuckness organized around relationship, fit, care, consent, and conflict. The gift is bridge-building. The distortion is self-erasing harmony: making things workable by removing one's own stake, need, boundary, or desire from the agreement.

Cards in this Door should help the player include themselves in the relationship, surface the real terms of contact, and turn conflict into workable accord without pressure or disappearance.

**Write against:** relationship, consent, repair, fit, contact, mutuality, agreement, bridge, belonging.

**Avoid:** fake peace, soft CTAs that avoid decisions, over-qualifying before the truth has a chance to land.

### Sage

The Sage Door is for stuckness organized around synthesis, maps, principles, and developmental perspective. The gift is coherence. The distortion is disembodied understanding: seeing the pattern so well that the player stays outside the risk of being changed by it.

Cards in this Door should help the player let wisdom become practice. The Sage card job is not to add more perspective. It is to locate the skipped embodied step beneath the beautiful explanation.

**Write against:** integration, principle, pattern, coherence, synthesis, developmental level, embodied step, what endures.

**Avoid:** more synthesis as avoidance, explaining before acting, mistaking perspective for participation.
