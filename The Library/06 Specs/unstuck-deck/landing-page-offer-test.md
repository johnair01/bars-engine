# Landing Page Offer Test

## Purpose

Test whether the market responds more strongly to pattern recognition, Door discovery, or self-compassion-through-signal framing.

## Hook Tests

| Test | Hero Line | CTA |
|---|---|---|
| Pattern-aware | For people who can name the pattern but still cannot move. | Find Your Door |
| Door-discovery | Find the Door under your stuckness. | Start the Quiz |
| Signal reframe | Stop treating stuckness as proof against yourself. | Read the Signal |

## MVP Offer

Quiz + 52-card digital deck + short guide + 321/BAR worksheets.

## Success Signals

- The visitor recognizes themselves in the first screen.
- The quiz feels useful before purchase.
- The deck feels like a guided practice journey, not a novelty card product.
- Buyers understand that the card is the interface and the practice loop is the offer.
