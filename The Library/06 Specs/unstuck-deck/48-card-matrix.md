# 48 Card Matrix

## Purpose

Define the 48 journey cards created by crossing the 6 Doors with the 8 Gates.

Use [[buyer-needs-card-requirements]] and [[buyer-needs-6gm-analysis]] as the buyer-need references, [[door-definitions]] as the Door reference, [[big-mind-voice-definitions]] and [[gate-language-review]] as the Gate references, and [[door-gate-writing-rules]] as the bridge layer while drafting card jobs and full cards.

## Door Arcs

Each Door can be played as an 8-Gate journey. The Gates move from protective management toward direct contact, growth, and integration.

| Door | Arc |
|---|---|
| Shaman | From overwhelmed attunement to trustworthy signal. |
| Challenger | From edited truth to precise courage. |
| Regent | From heroic bracing to sovereign stewardship. |
| Architect | From protective design to reality-tested structure. |
| Diplomat | From self-erasing harmony to honest accord. |
| Sage | From disembodied understanding to embodied integration. |

## Gate Jobs

Across all Doors, each Gate keeps a stable function.

| Gate | Stable Function |
|---|---|
| Protector | Moves from total armor to one specific protection, boundary, or defended value. |
| Controller | Identifies the standard being enforced and whether it serves life or control. |
| Skeptic | Converts undifferentiated doubt into one testable question. |
| Fixer | Redirects fixing from persons or identity to circumstances, skills, structures, or conditions. |
| Emotional Body | Locates the active EA channel and what it protects or reveals. |
| Victim | Moves from sympathy-seeking to one authorship move. |
| Damaged Self | Honors survival while distinguishing capacity from wound identity. |
| Vulnerable Child | Lets the unarmored learning/growth impulse lead one small move. |
