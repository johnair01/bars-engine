## Card Anatomy

Each sample card includes:

- Recognition
- Pattern Mirror
- Pattern Intelligence
- Cost
- Practice Move
- 321 Prompt
- BAR Capture

## 1. Shaman / Emotional Body

**Matrix Seed:** The whole field feels like noise and I cannot find the signal.

### Recognition

Where does everything feel alive at once?

The room, the person, the mood, the memory, the future, the body signal, the old wound, the thing nobody said. It may all arrive as one dense field of charge.

### Pattern Mirror

This is not proof that you are too sensitive.

It may mean the Emotional Body is receiving more signal than your system has sorted yet. The Shaman problem is not that you feel too much. The problem is that the signal has not become readable.

### Pattern Intelligence

The charge is trying to reveal what matters before your mind turns it into a story.

Fear may be orienting to risk. Sadness may be showing distance from what matters. Joy may be pointing toward aliveness. Anger may be clarifying a boundary. Neutrality may be trying to give the whole system balance.

### Cost

If the field stays undifferentiated, everything becomes noise.

Then you either armor against the whole thing, rescue what is not yours, or wait for certainty that never comes.

### Practice Move

Find the strongest body sensation.

Do not explain it yet. Locate it.

Then choose the least-wrong EA channel:

| Channel | Signal Question |
|---|---|
| Fear | What risk is my body orienting to? |
| Sadness | What matters that feels far away? |
| Joy | What aliveness is trying to return? |
| Anger | What boundary or clarity is asking for space? |
| Neutrality | What balance or distance is needed? |

Complete:

> The strongest signal is in my ____. It may be ____. It is trying to show me ____.

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at the charge as "that signal." What is that signal doing in the field?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the signal: "What are you trying to protect or reveal?"
- **1st person:** Become the signal and complete: "I am here because..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> When the whole field becomes noise, my first job is to find the channel.

### Integration Line

The body is giving you a signal, not a sentence.

## 2. Challenger / Vulnerable Child

**Matrix Seed:** My power wants somewhere real to grow, play, learn, explore, choose, and be met.

### Recognition

Where does your power want contact?

You may feel the urge to challenge, push, refuse, compete, test, win, or be met directly. That force may not be a problem. It may be the way this part learns.

### Pattern Mirror

This is not merely aggression.

At Challenger altitude, the Vulnerable Child grows through contact with power. It learns by testing strength, meeting resistance, trying the edge, refusing what does not fit, and discovering what it can become.

### Pattern Intelligence

The force is trying to develop you.

Challenge can be a playground, a classroom, and a proving ground. It can teach the young will how to choose, refuse, risk, recover, and be met.

### Cost

If challenge becomes only domination, the learning stops.

You may win the exchange but lose the growth. Or you may turn the force inward and challenge yourself so harshly that the part trying to learn stops playing.

### Practice Move

Choose one active challenge.

Complete:

> Through this challenge, I want to learn ____.

Then complete:

> The power I want to practice is ____.

Now choose a clean challenge move:

- make a direct ask,
- refuse one false term,
- enter the contest,
- take the attempt,
- name the standard,
- or ask to be met without softening.

Keep the move in service of growth, not victory alone.

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at "the challenge." What is it trying to teach?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the Vulnerable Child: "What do you want to learn through this power?"
- **1st person:** Become the Vulnerable Child and complete: "I grow when..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> My power can grow through the challenge.

### Integration Line

Let challenge become a place where the young force learns.

## 3. Regent / Fixer

**Matrix Seed:** I keep rescuing the system before it reveals the real break.

### Recognition

Where do you keep stepping in before the structure has to learn?

You may be the one who remembers, covers, repairs, follows up, smooths over, fills the gap, carries the tone, catches the miss, and makes sure the whole thing does not fall apart.

### Pattern Mirror

This may look like devotion, and some of it is.

But the Regent / Fixer pattern turns every break into a personal assignment. The Fixer patches the strained order so quickly that the missing structure never becomes visible.

### Pattern Intelligence

Rescue prevents immediate harm.

It protects dependents, promises, trust, continuity, and the dignity of what has been built. The part that fixes is not foolish. It has probably kept something real alive.

### Cost

If you keep rescuing the system, the system never has to reveal what it cannot hold.

Your body becomes the missing structure.

### Practice Move

Choose one recurring rescue.

Before you fix it again, complete:

> The recurring break is ____.

Then complete:

> The missing structure may be ____.

Choose one action that creates structure rather than another rescue:

- define a role,
- set a boundary,
- change a cadence,
- name an owner,
- remove a promise,
- or let the break be visible long enough to learn from it.

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at "the rescue." What does it keep from becoming visible?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the Fixer: "What are you afraid will happen if you stop patching this?"
- **1st person:** Become the Fixer and complete: "I keep rescuing this because..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> A recurring rescue is a structure asking to be built.

### Integration Line

Stewardship is not carrying what the structure refuses to learn.

## 4. Architect / Skeptic

**Matrix Seed:** Doubt keeps asking for a better model before I act.

### Recognition

Where are you asking for a better model before you let reality answer?

You may be refining the framework, improving the plan, clarifying the assumptions, naming the dependencies, and making the whole thing more elegant. And still, nothing has touched the world.

### Pattern Mirror

This is not laziness.

The Architect / Skeptic pattern turns uncertainty into model improvement. The Skeptic sees the weak assumptions. The Architect knows how to build around them. Together, they can postpone contact indefinitely.

### Pattern Intelligence

Doubt protects against wasted effort, weak assumptions, bad design, and public failure.

It wants the work to survive reality.

### Cost

If doubt only asks for a better model, reality never gets to answer.

The system becomes more coherent and less tested.

### Practice Move

Convert doubt into a testable question.

Complete:

> The question reality can answer this week is ____.

Then define the smallest test:

| Test Field | Answer |
|---|---|
| What will I try? |  |
| Who or what will provide feedback? |  |
| What will count as useful information? |  |
| When will I stop designing and run the test? |  |

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at "the model." What is it trying to prevent?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the Skeptic: "What is the specific failure point you want tested?"
- **1st person:** Become the Skeptic and complete: "I doubt this because..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> Doubt becomes useful when it designs the test.

### Integration Line

The whole map is not required for the next contact with reality.

## 5. Diplomat / Victim

**Matrix Seed:** I want sympathy for how much of myself I erased to keep the relationship.

### Recognition

Where do you want someone to finally understand how much of yourself you removed?

You may have made it easy. You may have stayed agreeable, flexible, generous, available, mature, careful, and fair. You may have preserved the relationship by disappearing from the terms of it.

### Pattern Mirror

The Victim voice here is not wrong for wanting sympathy.

Sympathy is real nourishment. The problem begins when sympathy becomes the whole goal and authorship stays offline.

### Pattern Intelligence

Self-erasure may have protected belonging.

It may have kept access to love, approval, safety, peace, or contact. The part seeking sympathy is trying to get the cost witnessed.

### Cost

If sympathy is the only goal, the agreement remains unchanged.

You may finally be understood as the one who disappeared, while still disappearing.

### Practice Move

Name the sympathy directly.

Complete:

> I want sympathy for ____.

Then reclaim one term of authorship:

> A term I accepted but did not choose is ____.

Now choose one next move:

- name the term,
- ask to renegotiate,
- make a decision,
- tell the truth,
- or stop calling an unchosen term an agreement.

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at "the agreement." Where did the self disappear?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the Victim voice: "What sympathy are you trying to receive?"
- **1st person:** Become the Victim voice and complete: "I want someone to understand..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> Agreement is not consent if I disappeared to keep it.

### Integration Line

Receive the sympathy, then recover authorship.

## 6. Sage / Protector

**Matrix Seed:** Witnessing has become armor against participation.

### Recognition

Where has perspective become the safest place to stand?

You may understand the pattern. You may see the developmental level, the archetype, the family system, the cultural force, the trauma logic, the principle underneath it all. And somehow, the seeing has not become participation.

### Pattern Mirror

This is not the absence of wisdom.

It may be the Protector using wisdom as armor. The witness seat protects coherence. It keeps the self from being changed by the very pattern it can name.

### Pattern Intelligence

Perspective prevents overwhelm.

It protects complexity, compassion, and the ability to stay with more than one truth at a time.

### Cost

If witnessing becomes armor, insight stays above the body.

You remain coherent, but untouched. You can explain the threshold without crossing it.

### Practice Move

Choose the embodied step your own insight requires.

Complete:

> If I actually entered the pattern I can see, I would ____.

Then make it smaller:

> The smallest embodied version of that is ____.

Do that version within 24 hours.

### 321 Prompt

- **3rd person:** Look at "the witness seat." What does it protect?
- **2nd person:** Speak to the Protector: "What are you armoring against by keeping me above this?"
- **1st person:** Become the Protector and complete: "I keep you coherent by..."

### BAR Capture

Write one phrase you could recognize next time:

> If I can see the pattern, I can enter one step.

### Integration Line

Let understanding descend into one embodied move.
