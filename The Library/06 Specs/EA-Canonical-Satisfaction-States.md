# EA Canonical Satisfaction States

The 5 EA channels each have exactly one canonical Satisfaction state — the Transcend endpoint when Dissatisfaction is held and transformed within the channel.

| Channel | Dissatisfaction | Satisfaction (Canonical Transcend Target) |
|---|---|---|
| **Fire/Anger** | Anger — burning at what harms, reactive heat | **Triumph** — anger refined into earned boundary, decisive action, victory |
| **Water/Sadness** | Sadness — contracting around loss, going inward | **Poignance** — grief that pierces but doesn't collapse; sharpness as medicine |
| **Metal/Fear** | Fear — contracting against threat, going still | **Excitement** — the same physiological energy as fear, but forward-moving; arousal without threat |
| **Wood/Joy** | Joy — reaching outward, expansion without ground | **Bliss** — expansion that has found its ground; abundance without grasping |
| **Earth/Neutrality** | Neutrality — frozen, structural failure, collapse | **Peace** — stillness that is full, not empty; the balanced field, equanimity |

## Key Distinctions

**Transcend** = valence shift within the same channel. Fear → Excitement (Metal). Anger → Triumph (Fire). Sadness → Poignance (Water). Joy → Bliss (Wood). Neutrality → Peace (Earth).

**Translate** = channel crossing. The energy leaves its current channel and crosses to a different element's territory via the Generation or Control cycle. Not a valence shift — an element shift.

## Generation Cycle (for Translate)

```
Metal → Water → Wood → Fire → Earth → Metal
```

**Translate/Metal→Fire:** Fear/Excess contraction → protective heat. The sharp edge of fear, held and contained, becomes the fire of right action.

**Translate/Water→Wood:** Sadness → growth. Grief, contained without collapse, becomes the depth that feeds new growth.

**Translate/Fire→Earth:** Anger → stability. The heat of anger, contained, burns away what is false and leaves only what is solid.
