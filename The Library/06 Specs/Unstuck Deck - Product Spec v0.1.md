# Unstuck Deck - Product Spec v0.1

## Core Thesis

The Unstuck Deck is not "another digital deck."

It is a card-guided practice system for people who can recognize the pattern but still cannot move.

The deck uses:

- **5 Emotional Alchemy channels** to locate the flavor of stuckness.
- **6 Doors** to locate the developmental stance or stuck style.
- **8 Gates / Big Mind voices** to move through the practice journey.
- **321** to deepen the encounter.
- **BAR capture** to preserve the insight as reusable pattern language.

The product should feel simple at the surface and deep underneath.

## Core Promise

> You are not stuck because you are broken. You are stuck because a real emotional signal got trapped inside a protective pattern.

The deck helps the player:

1. Name the emotional channel.
2. Find the Door organizing the stuckness.
3. Move through the 8 Gates.
4. Use 321 to face, speak to, and become the pattern.
5. Capture the insight as a BAR or practice seed.
6. Leave with one honest next move.

## Public Positioning

Working public line:

> A guided deck for people who can name the pattern but still cannot move.

Alternate lines:

- If you can feel the pattern but cannot find the door, start here.
- Stop treating stuckness as proof against yourself. Learn to read it as signal.
- The card-guided practice path for turning stuck patterns into honest next moves.
- Draw the card. Find the door. Meet the voice. Capture the shift.

## Ideal Client Avatar

### Primary Avatar

The primary buyer is the emotionally intelligent, pattern-aware person who is tired of turning every stuck place into proof that something is wrong with them.

They have probably tried some combination of:

- therapy,
- coaching,
- journaling,
- personality systems,
- spiritual practice,
- productivity systems,
- somatic work,
- shadow work,
- self-help books,
- meditation,
- online courses.

They are not lacking language. They are often drowning in language.

Their pain is:

> I can explain my problem beautifully and still not move.

### Trust Fit

This client trusts Wendell because he does not flatten the complexity.

They trust someone who can hold:

- emotional honesty,
- pattern recognition,
- spiritual/mythic framing,
- practical next moves,
- integral developmental nuance,
- game/deck/interface design,
- humor,
- shadow without humiliation,
- rigor without sterility.

The client is not looking for a minimalist productivity coach or a glossy self-help influencer.

They are looking for someone who can say:

> Yes, this is a pattern. Yes, it has a mythic shape. Yes, your body is involved. Yes, there is a practical next move. No, we are not going to flatten it.

## Anti-Avatar

This product is not primarily for:

- people who only want cute affirmation cards,
- people looking for instant certainty,
- people who want a purely clinical therapy replacement,
- people who want productivity hacks without emotional work,
- people who want mystical interpretation without practice,
- people who are not willing to reflect or act.

## Product Shape

### Working Name

**The Unstuck Deck**

Secondary / subtitle options:

- Six Doors, Eight Gates
- A Card-Guided MTGOA Practice Journey
- A Guided Deck for Pattern-Aware People Who Still Cannot Move
- A Practice Deck for Turning Stuckness into Signal

### Core Architecture

The deck has three nested layers:

1. **Emotional Channel** - What flavor of stuckness is active?
2. **Door** - What developmental stance or protective pattern is organizing it?
3. **Gate** - Which Big Mind voice needs to be met next?

This gives the product replay value:

> The same 8 Gates become different journeys depending on the Door and emotional channel.

## Canonical Emotional Alchemy Channels

| Channel | Element | Dissatisfied | Neutral | Satisfied |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fear | Metal | anxiety, panic, guarding, constriction | orientation | excitement, clarity, openness |
| Sadness | Water | grief, heaviness, distance, longing | acceptance | poignance, allowing, surrender |
| Joy | Wood | blocked joy, restless desire, manic reaching | appreciation | bliss, vitality, aliveness |
| Anger | Fire | frustration, rage, impatience | clarity | triumph, warmth, connection |
| Neutrality | Earth | apathy, numbness, flatness, stuck stability | balance | peace, grounded presence |

### Shame Clarification

Shame is not an EA channel.

Shame is what happens when the player identifies with the dissatisfaction state instead of reading it as signal.

The deck's job is not to "fix shame" directly. The deck helps the player translate stuckness back into signal so shame loosens its grip.

## The 6 Doors

The 6 Doors are the developmental stances. They are also market-facing stuck styles.

| Door | Stuck Style | Core Pain |
|---|---|---|
| Protector Shaman | Over-attuned signal confusion | I feel everything, but I do not trust what I am sensing. |
| Challenger | Edited truth | I know the truth, but I keep softening it. |
| Regent | Heroic over-responsibility | If I stop holding everything, I am afraid it falls apart. |
| Controller Architect | Protective system-building | I keep designing systems that protect me from the risk of moving. |
| Diplomat | Self-erasing bridge-building | I can see everyone's side except my own. |
| Sage | Disembodied understanding | I understand the pattern, but I have not embodied the next level. |

## The 8 Gates / Big Mind Voices

The 8 Gates are the journey sequence and Big Mind voice sequence.

Working Gate set from vault references:

1. Protector
2. Controller
3. Skeptic
4. Fixer
5. Fear
6. Victim
7. Damaged Self
8. Vulnerable Child

These are not merely "card topics." They are the voices/thresholds the player meets as they move through stuckness.

## Core Practice Loop

### Player Flow

1. **Arrive:** Player names where they feel stuck.
2. **Channel:** Player identifies the emotional channel or lets the product help classify it.
3. **Door:** Player chooses or draws one of the 6 Doors.
4. **Gate:** Player draws or proceeds through one of the 8 Gates.
5. **Card Practice:** Card gives a recognition prompt, pattern mirror, and practice move.
6. **321 Deepening:** Player faces the voice in 3rd, 2nd, and 1st person.
7. **BAR Capture:** Player captures the clearest pattern phrase or insight.
8. **Integration:** Player chooses one honest next move.

### UX Summary

At the surface:

> What kind of stuck are you?

Under the hood:

> EA channel + Door + Gate -> practice card -> 321 -> BAR seed -> next move.

## Card System

### Core Card Count

Recommended flagship structure:

- 48 core journey cards: 6 Doors x 8 Gates.
- 4 meta cards:
  - Orientation
  - 321 Capture
  - BAR Capture
  - Integration

Total: **52 cards**.

This 52-card structure is earned by the architecture. It is not decorative.

### Card Anatomy

Each journey card should include:

1. **Door + Gate**
   - Example: Controller Architect / Skeptic Gate

2. **Recognition Prompt**
   - A short question that helps the player recognize whether this card is active.

3. **Pattern Mirror**
   - A precise sentence naming the stuck structure.

4. **Practice Move**
   - One concrete action or inquiry.

5. **321 Prompt**
   - Face it, speak to it, become it.

6. **BAR Capture**
   - A prompt to extract one reusable phrase.

7. **Integration Line**
   - A sentence that carries the learning forward.

### Example Card

#### Controller Architect / Skeptic Gate

**Recognition:**  
Where are you asking for more certainty before you allow yourself to move?

**Pattern Mirror:**  
You may be using design as a way to postpone exposure to reality.

**Practice Move:**  
Name the smallest test that would give you real information in less than 24 hours.

**321 Prompt:**  
- 3rd person: What is "the system" trying to prevent?
- 2nd person: Ask the Skeptic what it needs before it will allow movement.
- 1st person: Become the Skeptic and complete: "I keep you safe by..."

**BAR Capture:**  
What phrase names the pattern clearly enough that you could recognize it next time?

**Integration:**  
A system that never meets reality is not structure. It is shelter.

> I know something is over, but I cannot cross into what comes next.

| Door | Pain State | Clickthrough Hook |
|---|---|---|

### Joy / Wood

| Challenger | I want more, but I keep turning desire into a fight. | Not every surge has to become a battle. Some are invitations. |
| Regent | I do not trust joy unless it has earned its place on the schedule. | A kingdom without vitality becomes a machine with nicer furniture. |
| Controller Architect | I keep optimizing the living thing until it stops breathing. | If the system kills the spark, the system is not serving the work. |
| Diplomat | I keep muting what I want so no one feels disrupted by my aliveness. | Your joy is not an inconvenience to manage. |
| Sage | I can explain what wants to grow, but I do not let myself be delighted by it. | Understanding aliveness is not the same as letting it move you. |

### Anger / Fire

Anger stuckness says:

> I know what is not okay, but I keep swallowing the heat.

| Door | Pain State | Clickthrough Hook |
|---|---|---|
| Protector Shaman | I feel the heat in the room but cannot tell whether it is mine, theirs, or old. | Your anger may be a boundary signal, not a character flaw. |

|---|---|---|

| Regent | Everything is stable, but nothing feels alive. | Stability is not the same as sovereignty. |

## Landing Page Spine

### Hero

**Headline:**  
For people who can name the pattern but still cannot move.

**Subhead:**  
The Unstuck Deck helps you translate stuckness into signal, enter through one of six Doors, move through the eight Gates, and capture the insight with 321 and BAR practice.

**CTA:**  
Find Your Door

### Pain Section

You are not short on insight.

You can explain the pattern. You can name the old wound. You can feel when something is off. You may even know what you "should" do next.

But some part of you still will not move.

That stuckness is not proof that you are broken. It is signal trapped inside a protective pattern.

### Mechanism Section

The deck works by helping you locate:

1. **The channel** - fear, sadness, joy, anger, or neutrality.
2. **The Door** - the developmental stance organizing the pattern.
3. **The Gate** - the Big Mind voice asking to be met.
4. **The practice** - the next honest move.

### Differentiation Section

This is not a prompt deck.

Prompt decks ask, "What do you think?"

The Unstuck Deck asks:

> What pattern is organizing your response, and what practice would let it move?

## Prototype Plan

### Phase 1 - Skeleton

- Define all 48 Door/Gate pairings.
- Draft 4 meta cards.
- Write one completed sample card per Door.
- Build self-sort quiz from the Unstuck Avatar Matrix.

### Phase 2 - Practice Test

- Give 5-10 trusted people the self-sort and 3-5 cards.
- Ask:
  - Did the pain language find you?
  - Did the card feel like a prompt or a practice?
  - Did the 321 deepen the experience?
  - Did you leave with a BAR-like phrase or next move?

### Phase 3 - Offer Test

- Build a simple landing page.
- Test three hooks:
  - "For people who can name the pattern but still cannot move."
  - "Find the Door under your stuckness."
  - "Stop treating stuckness as proof against yourself."

## Working Conclusion

The Unstuck Deck sells the thing Wendell already does:

> helping people stop treating stuckness as evidence against themselves and start reading it as a signal with a Door, a voice, and a practice.

The product should bring out the best of each Door while staying legible to the person who just knows:

> I have done enough work to recognize the pattern. Why am I still stuck?
