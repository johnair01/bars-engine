# Feature Request: Hub-and-Spoke Campaign Progression System

## Overview
This document outlines a proposal for a Hub-and-Spoke Campaign Progression System, incorporating Kotter's 8-Step Change Model to facilitate milestone-driven growth across multiple floors.

## Kotter's Stages
1. **Create Urgency**: Communicate the need for a new multi-floor growth strategy to stakeholders.
2. **Form a Powerful Coalition**: Assemble a team to drive the initiative.
3. **Create a Vision for Change**: Define what success looks like for the campaign and how it will impact growth.
4. **Communicate the Vision**: Share the vision widely and ensure everyone understands their role.
5. **Empower Action**: Remove barriers to change and encourage innovative ideas.
6. **Create Quick Wins**: Identify and celebrate early successes to build momentum.
7. **Build on the Change**: Use wins to drive further change and consolidate gains.
8. **Anchor the Changes**: Ensure that the new approaches are integrated into the organization’s culture.

## Hub-and-Spoke Model
- **Hub**: Central coordination point for strategies and communication.
- **Spokes**: Individual campaigns or initiatives that feed into the central hub, ensuring alignment and efficiency.

## Milestones for Multi-Floor Growth
- **Initial Assessment**: Evaluate current capabilities and opportunities for growth on each floor.
- **Pilot Launch**: Test the campaign on a small scale to gather data and insights.
- **Full Implementation**: Roll out the program across all floors, leveraging insights from the pilot.
- **Review and Reflect**: Regularly assess the progress against the milestones and adjust the strategy as needed.

## Conclusion
This feature request aims to establish a well-defined framework for the Hub-and-Spoke Campaign Progression System, leveraging Kotter's principles to drive sustainable multi-floor growth.
