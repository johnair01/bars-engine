The five elements & emotional alchemy

Every charge you capture is energy, not a verdict. Emotional alchemy is the grammar for metabolizing that energy — five elements, each a channel for a specific emotion, each carrying a lesson. It's an energy economy, not a morality wheel. Nothing here is good or bad; it's raw material waiting to move.

Charge = energy detected Element = which channel Alchemy = how it moves
01 · The channels

Each element channels one emotion

An emotion is a signal with information in it. The element tells you what kind of signal — and the lesson is what it's pointing at. Read the cards in the order energy naturally flows.

02 · The moves · WAVE + Open Up

Four moves metabolize a charge — and a fifth opens it

Within a single element, energy moves through four stages — the same moves you assign on a BAR. Notice the signal, correct the distortion, extract the lesson, then act on it. A fifth move — Open Up — wraps the others.

WWake Up
notice

Detect the emotional activation. Name that there's a charge here at all.

AClean Up
metabolize

Correct the distortion. Clarify the boundary, the risk, or the meaning.

VGrow Up
capacity

Extract the lesson. Integrate the insight so it stays with you.

EShow Up
act

Act aligned with the lesson. The charge becomes a move in the world.

O 5th move

Open Up

receive · soften

Before you correct it or act on it, open — toward the charge, toward the other, toward the ways of seeing you'd rather skip. Soften the armor; widen the aperture. It's the move that lets the other four land honestly instead of defensively.

Added from Ken Wilber's Finding Radical Wholeness — the fifth movement (Wake · Clean · Grow · Show · Open), opening to the Good, the True & the Beautiful in any situation.

03 · The vertical axis · altitude

Transcend — rising within a channel

Altitude is how metabolized a charge is, staying in the same emotion. Raw charge sits at dissatisfied; alchemy lifts it to satisfied. This is the glow you watched climb on the card.

Why it's in the intake

Every BAR is a charge waiting to move

When you capture, the card is raw — untuned, dissatisfied, no glow. Tuning names which element the charge belongs to (which emotion), picks the move that metabolizes it, and lifts its altitude. Do that and the card glows up to satisfied: a yellow brick paved, vibeulons minted. The intake screen is simply where the alchemy begins.
