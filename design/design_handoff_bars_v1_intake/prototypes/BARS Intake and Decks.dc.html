BARS Engine · Design Exploration

BAR intake & the deck system

A BAR begins as a blank card — anything you can scribble, paste, or speak, kept for safekeeping the moment inspiration arrives. The surface stays free. The structure comes later, when you tune the card into game-ready data.

01Capture · freedom

The blank card

Zero required fields. Write a line, sketch, paste a quote, snap a photo. Time, place & the sensation are captured silently as provenance.

02Tune · alchemy

Add the channels

Optional for keeping, required to play. Name the charge; assign element, altitude & a move. Each tap encodes the card further — a raw seed metabolizes into structured game data.

03Extend · output

Everywhere it travels

The same BAR becomes a quest, a deck entry, a printed card with a QR home, or a book-activity artifact. One capture, many products.

The map · read this first

From captured idea to game artifact

A BAR captures a charge — an emotional kernel marked by element and satisfaction. Capture is the MVP and stays free-form. But tuning is the gate: a BAR can't enter the game until it's attuned. The core loop alchemizes dissatisfied charge into satisfied through play; inspiration arrives already satisfied, ready to bloom into an artifact. Provenance is locked at capture and never lost.

Time, place & the sensation are locked at capture. Attuning or growing a BAR changes its layout — and a bloomed artifact looks nothing like the seed — but every state keeps a thread back to the original spark. You can always return to where the idea came from.
