# Spec: Gameboard and Campaign Deck — Onboarding Blocker

## Purpose

Implement the gameboard as the surface where campaign quests are completed. The game map transcends quests and adventures (structural, experiential); the gameboard is where campaign work happens. Each period, 8 quests are drawn from the campaign deck onto the gameboard. Players complete them there, spend vibeulons to convert cards to subquests or add custom subquests, and completed slots are replaced by new draws. This unblocks the onboarding flow.

## Priority

- **Blocker** for onboarding flow
- **Lower priority** than I Ching quests and grammatical content in autogenerated test quests

## Context / Goal

The quest completion context restriction (CT) blocks campaign quest completion outside the gameboard. The gameboard UI and campaign deck mechanics do not yet exist. Players need a place to complete campaign quests and interact with the shared campaign space.

## Conceptual Model (Game Language)

- **WHO**: Player (completer, spender), Campaign (quest source)
- **WHAT**: Gameboard = 8 slots; Campaign deck = pool of campaign quests; Cards = quests on slots
- **WHERE**: Gameboard (e.g. `/campaign` or `/campaign/board`); Map transcends and contains it
- **Energy**: Vibeulons — spent to convert card to subquest or add custom subquest
- **Personal throughput**: Draw → Complete → Replace; Spend vibeulons → Subquest actions

## User Stories

### P1: Campaign deck draws onto gameboard

**As the system**, I want 8 quests drawn from the campaign deck onto the gameboard each period, so that players see current campaign work.

**Acceptance**: Each period (GlobalState.currentPeriod or instance.kotterStage—TBD), 8 slots are populated from the campaign deck. Deck = quests with campaignRef or in campaign thread.

### P2: Completion replaces with new draw

**As a player**, I want a completed quest slot to be replaced by a new draw from the campaign deck, so the gameboard stays active.

**Acceptance**: When a quest on the gameboard is completed (source: gameboard), that slot is cleared and a new quest is drawn from the deck. Completion mints vibeulons per existing quest-engine logic.

### P3: Spend vibeulon to convert card to subquest

**As a player**, I want to spend a vibeulon to make a card on the gameboard a subquest of the gameboard, so I can nest work under the campaign.

**Acceptance**: Player spends 1 vibeulon; the quest becomes a subquest of the gameboard container (or equivalent). Card remains on board as subquest.

### P4: Spend vibeulon to add custom subquest

**As a player**, I want to spend a vibeulon to add a custom subquest to the gameboard, so I can contribute my own work to the campaign.

**Acceptance**: Player spends 1 vibeulon; creates or attaches a custom quest as subquest of the gameboard. New card appears on board.

### P5: Gameboard completion source

**As a player**, I want to complete campaign quests on the gameboard, so that completion is allowed (CT restriction permits source: gameboard).

**Acceptance**: Gameboard UI calls `completeQuest` with `source: 'gameboard'`. Campaign quests complete successfully.

## Functional Requirements

### FR1: Campaign deck definition

- Campaign deck = quests eligible for draw. Criteria: (a) in ThreadQuest whose thread has adventure with campaignRef, or (b) CustomBar with campaign tag/instance. Exact criteria TBD from schema.
- Deck is instance/campaign-scoped (e.g. instance.campaignRef or campaignRef param).

### FR2: Gameboard slots (8 per period)

- Model: `GameboardSlot` or equivalent (instanceId, period, slotIndex, questId, drawnAt). Or derive from existing schema.
- 8 slots per period. Period = GlobalState.currentPeriod or instance.kotterStage (specify in plan).
- On period advance or first load: draw 8 from deck, fill empty slots.

### FR3: Draw on completion

- When quest on gameboard completes: clear slot, draw 1 from deck, place in slot.
- Draw excludes quests already on board this period.

### FR4: Vibeulon spend — convert to subquest

- Action: `convertGameboardCardToSubquest(slotId, playerId)`. Cost: 1 vibeulon.
- Effect: Quest becomes subquest of gameboard container. Deduct vibeulon from player.

### FR5: Vibeulon spend — add custom subquest

- Action: `addCustomSubquestToGameboard(playerId, questInput?)`. Cost: 1 vibeulon.
- Effect: Create or attach custom quest as subquest; add to board (or next slot). Deduct vibeulon.

### FR6: Gameboard route and UI

- Route: `/campaign` (existing) or `/campaign/board` (new). Renders 8 slots, completion actions, spend actions.
- Completion action passes `source: 'gameboard'` to `completeQuest`.

## Non-functional Requirements

- Integrate with quest-completion-context-restriction (CT). Gameboard is the only place campaign quests complete.
- Vibeulon spend uses existing economy (VibeulonLedger, VibulonEvent, or equivalent).

## Non-goals (this iteration)

- Full map visualization (story-quest-map-exploration)
- I Ching quest grammatical fixes (higher priority, separate)
- Adventure completion record (CU, deferred)

- [Quest Completion Context Restriction](quest-completion-context-restriction/spec.md) (CT) — implemented; gameboard must pass source: gameboard
- Campaign deck seed/source — may need admin or seed to populate deck
