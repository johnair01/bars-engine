Two design calls left

Confirm before I build

A

Add-a-lead: quick vs. full?

I’m proposing a 4-field quick-add that drops you into the Workspace, retiring the big forge form. Or keep the full forge form as the add step.

Lean: quick-add → Workspace.
B

Adopt = copy or shared reference?

Adopting a collective lead clones it (each steward owns their copy + goals). Alternative: a shared lead many campaigns reference. Copy is simpler and keeps notes private.

Lean: copy.

Build order

  1. Schema + actions. Add goals, collective; migration. New actions: quickAddLead, setLeadGoals, addLeadQuest, reorderLeadQuests, removeLeadQuest, publishLeadToCollective/unpublishLead, adoptCollectiveLead, getLead.
  2. Roster. Reframe the board as “your list” + quick-add modal + Collective filter chip.
  3. Workspace /leads/[leadId]. Goals autosave, quest add/reorder/remove, link copy + preview, publish toggle.
  4. Collective /leads/collective — browse + adopt.
  5. Verify. tsc / eslint / validate:routes green; the welcome link + claimCampaignLeadForPlayer still assign quests end-to-end.
