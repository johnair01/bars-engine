The Quest Studio

Where a campaign owner authors a custom quest by aiming three lenses at once — an allyship myth, a superpower, and a Game-Master face. The three converge through one shared vocabulary; the AI drafts from that seed; you edit and save it to the campaign, ready to match onto any lead.

The one idea

Three lenses, one quest

You decided quests should be AI-populated and aligned with the myths, the superpower intakes, and the six-face GM games. The Studio makes that a single act: pick the lenses, watch them compose into a seed, let the AI draft a real quest from it, edit, save. The saved quest joins the campaign pool — so it shows up in the “Add from campaign quests” picker on every lead’s workspace (and finally lights up the “Author new…” button we stubbed in Phase 6).

The Six Faces & their 48 moves

Design calls before build

Confirm these

A

Face → move: auto-stage or pick the move?

Pick just the face and let the opening move come from the campaign’s current Kotter stage (simpler), or pick face + stage to target one exact move of the 48.

Lean: face only; stage auto from the campaign.
B

Alignment: columns or JSON blob?

Explicit nullable columns on CustomBar (gmFace, superpowerAffinity, mythId) make “all connector quests” a real query. A JSON blob is looser but unqueryable.

Lean: explicit columns.
C

AI default: draft-first or assemble-first?

You chose AI-populated. Open with the AI draft by default, or show the deterministic assembly first and offer “✦ Draft with AI” as the upgrade?

Lean: AI draft by default; assembly is the fallback.
D

Who can author? (scope)

Steward+ of the campaign (matches the rest of Lead Forge), or global admins only (like the existing /admin/quest-from-context)?

Lean: steward+ (campaign owners), consistent with Phase 6.

Build order

  1. Alignment lib + schema. quest-alignment.ts (composeAlignmentSeed, assembleAlignedQuest — both pure, unit-tested). Add gmFace/superpowerAffinity/mythId columns; migration.
  2. Actions. draftAlignedQuest (wraps generateQuestFromContext with the seed), saveAlignedQuest, listCampaignQuests, archiveQuest — steward-gated.
  3. Composer /quests/new — lens selectors, live seed, draft/assemble, edit, save. basePath-threaded + the-crossing shim (same pattern as Phase 6).
  4. Library /quests — authored quests + tags, edit/archive.
  5. Wire Phase 6. Light up the workspace “Author new…” button → Composer with ?forLead=; on save, offer to add to that lead.
  6. Verify. tsc / eslint / validate:routes green; AI path behind a flag with the deterministic fallback proven offline.
