## Meta-Structure

Each BAR is an artifact of lived experience encoded as polarity.

Format:
- **BAR**
- **Type** (when relevant)
- **Polarity (A ↔ B)**
- **Tension**
- **Shadow A / Shadow B**
- **Why it matters**

This is not a static taxonomy. Entries may evolve, merge, split, or contradict as the system matures.
