## Awake signal loop (development strategy)

Increase **throughput** on player pain by **gamifying** how we **sense** and **respond**: feedback is not only ops overhead—it is woven into the **same story** as the core loop. Players **sense charge** → **collect** it → **process** via **321** → **create** a quest or **campaign seed** → **complete** and receive an **artifact** and another **BAR** to continue. The **system** should be **awake** to user needs in parallel with users becoming more awake to their own—**Share Your Signal**, **site-signal (nav)**, and future in-world **signal quests** feed **cert-feedback-triage** → specs/backlog without breaking immersion.
