# The Witness Turn — Resolving Inner/Outer in Raise Awareness

> **The seed (Wendell):** "Honest expression of the inner as content solves this. Doing the
> inner work and telling people about it IS an outer expression." This doc pushes that into a
> resolution and shows it's already half-encoded in the deck — and that resolving it fixes the
> external Raise Awareness moves, not just the inner ones.

---

## 1. The tension (as it stood)

The prior review split the RA domain into **outer** cards (Wake/Show — raise awareness of an
external truth) and **inner** cards (Open/Clean/Grow — metabolize the self). The two-context
test (MTGOA / Cascade) then "failed" the inner cards: witnessing a truth for three breaths
raises no one's awareness, so it looked like practitioner-prep, not content.

That split is real but **mis-drawn**. It treats inner/outer as a property of the *move*. It
isn't. It's a property of whether the work **stays private or gets expressed.**

---

## 2. The resolution — the metabolize → express arc

**Raising awareness has two possible contents — the world, and the self-in-process — and the
second becomes outer through honest expression.** One move, two directions the *content* can
face:

- **Point** (exo-awareness): make an **external truth** visible. *"Here is what's unseen / what
  must change."* Native to Wake (see it) and Show (say/act it).
- **Witness** (endo-awareness): make your **honest inner process** visible. *"Here is what
  doing this work surfaced in me."* Native to Open / Clean / Grow — **once expressed.**

Both are **outer**. Both **raise awareness**. The deck currently ships **Point** as "external"
and mis-files **Witness** as "private inner work." **Promoting Witness to a first-class outer
register is the whole resolution.** Inner/outer was never a wall — it was a **pipeline**:
metabolize the charge (inner) → produce a BAR → **express the BAR** (outer). Expression is the
turn.

> Inner and outer are not two kinds of card. They are two ends of one arc, hinged on the BAR.

---

## 3. It's already half-encoded in the deck (three hooks)

This isn't a bolt-on; the ontology already leans this way:

1. **The BAR flow is an expression pipeline.** Every move metabolizes charge into a **BAR**
   (awareness/experience/insight/wisdom/artifact — `move-library.ts`, `glossary.ts`). A BAR is
   *the expressible unit.* Inner work is not private-forever; it yields an artifact that **can
   be told.** Show Up (invest capacity → artifact) is literally the expression move. So an
   inner card completes its outer arc by being **Shown Up** — expressed.
2. **The cards already name the enemy of Witness: performance.** Across the RA set the failure
   modes are *"Performing awareness"* (WAKE-RA-SHAMAN), *"Performing feeling"* (OPEN-RA-SHAMAN),
   *"Performing care"* (OPEN-RA-DIPLOMAT), *"Performing growth"* (GROW-RA-SHAMAN), *"Performative
   one-offs"* / *"Performative calm"* (SHOW/CLEAN). The deck already knows the danger of
   expressed-inner-as-content is **performing it instead of honestly witnessing it.** The
   honesty ↔ performance polarity is *already in the content* — we're just promoting it to an
   axis.
3. **The `aspect` model + Subject toggle gesture at it.** `quest-grammar/move-aspect.ts` (built)
   says every move has inner/outer expressions; the deck's `primaryQuestion` vs
   `campaignQuestion` toggle is a coarse self/other reading. Neither yet encodes **Witness** —
   the outer expression whose *content is the inner*.

---

## 4. The honesty gate (the real teeth)

Witness raises awareness **only when it's honest testimony, not performance.** This is the
governing polarity for the register — a sibling to Calm ↔ Progress in
`VALUES_AND_POLARITIES.md`:

| Pole | Witness as… | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Honest** | first-person testimony of metabolized work | raises awareness; models the practice; builds trust |
| **Performed** | broadcasting an inner state you haven't actually done | the failure modes the cards already name |

So the new **fail case is not "it's inner."** It's **"the only available expression would be
performance"** — which happens exactly when the work hasn't been done. That's a *truer* gate: a
card fails if expressing it would be performing, not testifying.

---

## 5. Why this fixes the *external* RA moves (the payoff)

The external awareness moves (`SHOW-RA-CHALLENGER` Say the Thing, `SHOW-RA-ARCHITECT` Build the
Megaphone, `SHOW-RA-SHAMAN` Aim the Awareness) fail into their own warned failure modes —
**rage-posting, dumping charge on an audience, preaching, purity tone, performative awareness**
— precisely when they express **un-metabolized** content. Point without Witness is preachy.

The inner cards are the **metabolization that makes Point honest.** Open/Clean/Grow-RA aren't a
separate track from the external moves — they're the **fuel that makes external expression land
instead of preach.** Reunite them and the external RA moves get their teeth back. That is the
"fixes a lot of what's an issue inside the raise awareness external moves" you felt.

**And the community payoff:** honest first-person Witness is the **least AI-replicable, most
trust-building** content there is. A language model can fake a Point ("here's a fact about
injustice"); it cannot fake *your* honest inner process. In a community allergic to AI, Witness
is the moat. The ontology resolution and the marketing strategy are the same move.

---

## 6. The two-context test, reframed

Not "does an external awareness move pop?" but: **"What is the honest expression of this card's
work — as content — for MTGOA and Cascade Camp?"** A worked card always pops, in one register:

- **Point** → the external truth it surfaces.
- **Witness** → the honest inner process it makes shareable.
- **Fail** → the only expression available would be **performed** (⇒ the work isn't done, or the
  card can't be honestly expressed in that context).

---

## 7. The slice, demonstrated (drafts — candidates for your voice)

| Card | Aspect / Register | MTGOA expression | Cascade Camp expression | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `WAKE-RA-ARCHITECT` "The Story That Could Land" | outer / **Point** | Name the one reframe that changes the most: *"You're not a bad ally — you're an unpracticed one."* | Name the one thing a camper actually leaves with, to the people who'd want exactly that. | **Pass (Point)** |
| `SHOW-RA-CHALLENGER` "Say the Thing" | outer / **Point** | Say the specific claim you keep softening, today, to your followers. | Post the specific ask from the podcast you keep hedging. | **Pass (Point)** |
| `OPEN-RA-SHAMAN` "What Seeing Costs" | inner / **Witness** | *"Here's what it actually cost me to see my own gap as an ally — I felt ___. Naming that is the work."* | *"Here's the moment it landed what this camp is really for — and what it asked of me."* | **Pass (Witness)** — was a "fail" under the old test |
| `GROW-RA-SHAMAN` "The Witness Growing in You" | inner / **Witness** | *"The capacity I'm growing right now is saying the hard thing without softening. Here's where I'm at."* (build-in-public) | *"The capacity we're growing as organizers is ___ — here's the rep we're on."* | **Pass (Witness)** |

Under the old test, two of four failed. Under the resolution, **all four pass** — two as Point,
two as Witness — and the teeth move to *honest vs performed.* The inner cards are rescued as
the deck's most authentic content, not dead weight.

---

## 8. Proposed ontological additions

1. **`ExpressionRegister = 'point' | 'witness'`** — a first-class tag on how an RA card raises
   awareness. Derivable deterministically (no AI) from move-aspect: Wake/Show-native → Point-
   capable; Open/Clean/Grow → Witness-primary. A card may afford both.
2. **Promote the honesty ↔ performance polarity** into `VALUES_AND_POLARITIES.md` as the gate on
   Witness (the cards' "Performing X" failure modes become its shadow pole).
3. **Wire it to the deck, not just quests:** give each RA card its register(s); the Subject
   toggle becomes the *surface* of aspect; the practice engine's `orientation` aligns to it.
4. **The core loop this reveals:** metabolize charge (inner) → BAR → **witness/point** (outer) →
   the expression is itself a compostable artifact *and* raises others' awareness *and* models
   the practice that teaches them to metabolize. That is the engine's flywheel — content,
   pedagogy, and marketing moat are one loop ("the game creates the game").

---

## 9. What changes for the RA improvement pass

- The ~18 "inner" cards are **not** prep to be labeled and sidelined. They are **Witness
  content** — draft their applications as honest first-person testimony, not fake external
  moves.
- The ~12 "outer" cards get **Point** applications — but grounded (their honesty comes from the
  Witness work behind them).
- The audit's `applications` gap stands; but each card's two applications are now typed by
  **register** (Point or Witness), and the QA gate adds one check: **could this be said honestly,
  or only performed?**
