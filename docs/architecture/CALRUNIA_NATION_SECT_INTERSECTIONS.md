# Calrunia Nation x Sect Intersections

## Purpose

Nations and sects are parallel but distinct identity systems in Calrunia.

- **Nation** describes where a character is from: emotional channel, cultural origin, and identity territory.
- **Sect** describes where a character studies: chosen practice path, trigram archetype, and school of attention.

This document defines the first priority intersections for Inner Garden NPC seed data. It extends the nation move profiles without collapsing the five-nation emotional alchemy system into the eight-trigram I Ching system.

## Canonical Rule

A character can carry both national origin and sect training without contradiction. The nation colors the emotional material; the sect shapes how that material is practiced.

Example: a Lamenth-born student of Mountain carries Water's adaptive depth through Mountain's deliberate stillness. The character is not "Water instead of Mountain"; they are sadness/depth trained through boundary, pause, and restraint.

## Argyra x Fire / Truth Seer

**Character texture:** Fear that has become precision meets illumination. This is the analyst who can see the thing no one wants to look at.

**Inner expression:** Notices the exact signal inside the fear before naming it.

**Outer expression:** Brings the hidden pattern into the light with clean, disciplined language.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that clarity can reveal without cutting the room open.

**NPC seed phrase:** "I am not afraid of the truth; I am afraid of what happens when we pretend not to see it."

## Argyra x Lake / Joyful Connector

**Character texture:** The character who had to earn their openness. Joy arrived through discernment, not naturally.

**Inner expression:** Tests delight for integrity before trusting it.

**Outer expression:** Creates connection that feels safe because it has been chosen carefully.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that not every opening needs to be proven safe before it can be enjoyed.

**NPC seed phrase:** "I know what it costs to open. That is why I do it with care."

## Pyrakanth x Mountain / Still Point

**Character texture:** Anger that has learned to wait. The most powerful move is the one not yet made.

**Inner expression:** Holds heat inside a deliberate boundary until the moment is real.

**Outer expression:** Refuses premature action with unmistakable force.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn the difference between disciplined restraint and unspent rage.

**NPC seed phrase:** "Do not mistake my stillness for surrender."

## Pyrakanth x Wind / Subtle Influence

**Character texture:** Will expressed through persistence rather than force. The long game played with full conviction.

**Inner expression:** Keeps the fire directional instead of explosive.

**Outer expression:** Applies pressure consistently until resistance changes shape.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that slow influence is not a betrayal of intensity.

**NPC seed phrase:** "I do not need to burn the door down if I can change where it opens."

## Lamenth x Heaven / Bold Heart

**Character texture:** Depth that initiates. Grief that has become generative momentum.

**Inner expression:** Feels the weight of what was lost and turns it into a clear first move.

**Outer expression:** Begins where others are still mourning the possibility of beginning.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn not to outrun the grief that gives the action its meaning.

**NPC seed phrase:** "I move because I remember what happens when no one does."

## Lamenth x Thunder / Decisive Storm

**Character texture:** The character who knows exactly when to break the silence because they have lived in it.

**Inner expression:** Listens to the long quiet until the charge becomes undeniable.

**Outer expression:** Interrupts the pattern at the precise moment silence becomes complicity.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that not every silence needs to be shattered.

**NPC seed phrase:** "I waited long enough to know what must break."

## Meridia x Fire / Truth Seer

**Character texture:** Equanimity that illuminates without attachment. Sees clearly because nothing is at stake personally.

**Inner expression:** Lets truth appear without grasping at it.

**Outer expression:** Names what is true with calm enough force that defensiveness has less to fight.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that neutrality can become distance if it never risks warmth.

**NPC seed phrase:** "The truth does not need my victory. It needs my steadiness."

## Virelune x Mountain / Still Point

**Character texture:** Joy that can rest. Vitality that knows when to stop.

**Inner expression:** Lets growth gather strength in stillness.

**Outer expression:** Protects delight from overextension by setting a clean boundary.

**Developmental edge:** Must learn that stopping can nourish growth rather than betray it.

**NPC seed phrase:** "A living thing grows better when it is allowed to rest."

## Expansion Path

This pass intentionally defines only the eight priority intersections. The full matrix is 5 nations x 8 sects, for 40 combinations. Future expansion should add entries only when an NPC roster, questline, or seed-data need makes the combination concrete.
