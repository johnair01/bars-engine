# Five Move Metabolism

## ⭐ Core Insight

The Five Moves are not categories of development.

They are capacities that can be applied to any domain.

More specifically:

> The Five Moves are stages in the metabolism of charge.

---

## The Recursive Cycle

Open Up
↓
Wake Up
↓
Clean Up
↓
Grow Up
↓
Show Up
↓
Open Up

The cycle repeats continuously.

---

## Open Up

Encounter new reality.

A dream.
A relationship.
A possibility.
A challenge.

Output:

New charge.

---

## Wake Up

Notice the charge.

Capture the signal.

Create a BAR Seed.

---

## Clean Up

Investigate the charge.

Meet the daemon.

Surface the belief.

Reduce distortion.

---

## Grow Up

Expand perspective.

Understand the pattern.

Distill a mature BAR.

---

## Show Up

Act.

Create reality.

Generate consequences.

---

## Domain vs Move Distinction

⭐ Domains tell us where transformation happens.

⭐ Moves tell us how transformation happens.

Domains answer:

> What game am I playing?

Examples:

- Gathering Resources
- Raise Awareness
- Direct Action
- Skillful Organizing

Moves answer:

> How am I playing it?

- Wake Up
- Clean Up
- Grow Up
- Show Up
- Open Up

---

## Major Consequence

Every meaningful domain requires all five moves.

The weighting changes.

The capacities do not.

A resource quest requires:

- Wake Up to available resources
- Clean Up resistance to receiving
- Grow Up understanding of systems
- Show Up action
- Open Up to support

The same principle applies across all domains.
