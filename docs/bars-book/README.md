# BARs Book

This folder tracks the emerging manuscript and conceptual spine for the BARs book.

## Working thesis

BARs are not merely notes, tasks, or productivity artifacts.

A BAR is a **compressed soul artifact**: a small, portable container for a moment of meaningful impact. It preserves orientation, not just information.

The BARs practice helps a person carry important knowledge literally on hand so they can recover their path when they lose the plot, complete 52-card sprints, and teach others to recognize moments worth carrying forward with the phrase: **“That’s a BAR.”**

## Current manuscript direction

The book should not begin as a grand cosmology of BARs. The reader needs a first victory: their first deck, their first map, their first return to themselves.

The deeper promise is:

> Keep a record of what matters so that when you inevitably lose the trail, you can find your way back to it.
