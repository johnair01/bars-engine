# The Daemon → BAR Workflow

## Purpose

Transform stalled momentum into wisdom, action, and compound growth.

---

## Workflow

### 1. Active Quest

A meaningful goal, intention, campaign, or adventure is active.

Output: Direction and intention.

---

### 2. Charge Capture

Momentum stalls.

Capture:

- What was I trying to do?
- Where did I stop?
- What story appeared?
- What did I feel?
- What did I do instead?
- What did it cost me?

Output: Structured charge.

---

### 3. Spawn Daemon

When charge is high and action is low, a daemon emerges.

Output: Daemon seed.

---

### 4. Select Game Master Lens

Choose:

- Shaman
- Challenger
- Regent
- Architect
- Diplomat
- Sage

Output: Interpretation and insight.

---

### 5. Identify Belief and Function

Identify:

- Core self-sabotaging belief
- Momentum-management function

Output: Belief + Function.

---

### 6. Understand the Daemon

Map:

- Strategy
- Pressure managed
- Tradeoff
- Behavior loop
- False promise
- Actual result

Output: Full daemon profile.

---

### 7. Choose Your Move

Select the GM-guided counter-move.

Output: Aligned action.

---

### 8. Take Action

Act despite the belief.

Output: New data.

---

### 9. Extract the BAR

Distill the experience.

Question:

> What is the portable wisdom from this encounter?

Output: BAR.

---

### 10. Integrate and Compound

Apply the BAR.

Track recurrence.

Transform repeated daemon encounters into allied functions.

Output: Growth and leverage.

---

## Foundational Insight

A daemon is a belief wearing armor.

A BAR is the wisdom left behind when the armor falls off.

---

## Relationship to BARs

This workflow formalizes one of the primary BAR excavation loops.

Beliefs generate charge.
Charge generates daemons.
Daemons reveal beliefs.
Beliefs are transformed into BARs.
BARs generate quests, adventures, campaigns, and future action.
