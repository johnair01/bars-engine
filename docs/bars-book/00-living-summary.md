# BARs Book — Living Summary

## Canonical BAR

> When something matters, give it a place to live.

This appears to be a load-bearing BAR for the entire system.

## What is a BAR?

Current working definition:

A BAR is a compressed soul artifact.

A BAR preserves orientation, not merely information.

It is a portable container for a moment, insight, relationship, object, quest, inner part, or experience that left a meaningful impact on the shape of a person's map.

## The Recognition Event

A person says "That's a BAR" when they encounter:

1. Compression — a large territory folded into a small artifact.
2. Charge — a felt sense of recognition or significance.
3. Generativity — the artifact unfolds into further territory when explored.

## The Blue Sky Hypothesis

BARs may have emerged as a response to a period of perspectival overload.

The wound was not loss of memory.

The wound was loss of center.

The function of BARs is not merely remembering.

The function of BARs is recovering orientation.

Working opening line:

> BARs is the map I wish I had carried with me.
