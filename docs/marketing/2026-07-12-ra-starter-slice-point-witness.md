## The process rule this slice establishes

| Register | How AI drafts it | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Point** (external truth) | full candidate copy — you edit for voice | the content is a claim about the world; I can propose it, you own the wording |
| **Witness** (honest inner process) | **scaffold + elicitation prompt only** — you supply the lived detail | AI-generated "inner experience" is fabricated → the performed shadow → fails the honesty gate |

This is the AI-allergy guardrail, sharpened by the ontology. Apply it to all 30.

## 1. `WAKE-RA-ARCHITECT` — "The Story That Could Land" · **Point** (primary)
*Move: Wake Up (see). Card's move: "Name the one fact that, if everyone saw it, would change things."*

- **MTGOA (Point) — candidate:** "The one fact that changes everything: allyship is a **skill you
  practice**, not a **virtue you have**. People fail at it the way anyone fails an unpracticed
  skill — no reps, not bad character. Say that, and the shame drops and the practice can start."
- **Cascade Camp (Point) — candidate (needs your real outcome):** "The one fact that would change
  who signs up: [the specific transformation a camper actually leaves with — e.g. 'people arrive
  as attendees and leave having run their own session']. Say *that* fact, not 'it's a great
  community.'"
- **Honesty check:** the fact must be true and load-bearing. MTGOA = your thesis (solid). Cascade
  = must come from a real camper outcome, not a brochure line. **Verdict: PASS both** (Cascade
  pending the real outcome).

## 2. `SHOW-RA-CHALLENGER` — "Say the Thing" · **Point** (the 7/7 template)
*Move: Show Up (express). Card's move: "Say the thing today — specifically, to the people who need to hear it."*

- **MTGOA (Point) — candidate:** "You keep softening it. Say it straight today: '[the exact
  sentence you've been hedging — e.g. "You're not a bad ally. You're an unpracticed one. Here's
  your first rep."]' — to the people who follow you *because* they want to be better and don't
  know how."
- **Cascade Camp (Point) — candidate:** "Stop hinting. Say the specific ask from the last podcast:
  '[the one concrete thing — register / volunteer / bring one person]' — name who, name by when."
- **Honesty check:** the softened sentence must be one you *actually believe and have been
  avoiding*. Fail = manufacturing a hot take you don't hold. **Verdict: PASS both.**

## 3. `OPEN-RA-SHAMAN` — "What Seeing Costs" · **Witness** (only)
*Move: Open Up (receive/feel). Card's move: "Let the truth land for one breath before you respond."*
**Scaffold — your lived detail goes in the brackets. I am deliberately not inventing the feeling.**

- **MTGOA (Witness) — scaffold:** "The first time I really let it land that I'd [specific moment
  you missed something / failed as an ally], I felt [the actual feeling — shame / grief /
  defensiveness, in your body]. I'm telling you because [what it taught you about the practice].
  Seeing costs something. That cost *is* the work."
  - *Elicitation:* What's one moment that seeing your own gap actually cost you — and what did you
    feel? Tell that, plainly.
- **Cascade Camp (Witness) — scaffold:** "When I let myself actually see what this community is
  holding — [the real thing] — it cost me [the honest feeling]. Here's what shifted."
- **Honesty check:** passes ONLY if it's a real moment you can name. If you'd have to invent it,
  the card **fails for that context** — don't perform it. **Verdict: PASS both, if the moments are
  real** (they almost certainly are — this is the most authentic content you have).

## 4. `GROW-RA-SHAMAN` — "The Witness Growing in You" · **Witness** (only)
*Move: Grow Up (develop). Card's move: "Name the seeing or speaking capacity one size bigger than today."*
**Scaffold — build-in-public register.**

- **MTGOA (Witness) — scaffold:** "The capacity I'm growing right now is [the one-size-bigger skill
  — e.g. 'saying the hard thing without softening it to mush']. I'm not there. Here's the rep I'm
  on and where I keep slipping: [your honest current edge]. I'm telling you because watching
  someone *practice* is more useful than watching someone perform mastery."
  - *Elicitation:* What awareness/allyship capacity are you actively, imperfectly growing *this
    week*? Name the edge you keep hitting.
- **Cascade Camp (Witness) — scaffold:** "The capacity we're growing as organizers is [X] — here's
  the rep we're on together."
- **Honesty check:** the shadow is **"performing growth"** (a failure mode *this exact card*
  names). A polished "here's how I grew" story = fail. "Here's where I'm stuck right now" = pass.
  **Verdict: PASS both, if you name a real current edge** (not a humblebrag).
